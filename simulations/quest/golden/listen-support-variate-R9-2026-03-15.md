**R9 questions:**

   site? Does leaving it unregistered in RESTATING POSITIONS reveal a registry-completeness
   gap? (V-01)

   where the manifest both verifies compliance and restates the governing rule? (V-02)
3. Does upgrading STEP 3B and STEP 4 instructions to direct consequence-form text (in
   addition to RESTATING POSITIONS back-references that already carry it) create double-
   anchored enforcement independently verifiable without consulting the contract? (V-03)
4. Do STEP 6 enforcement + RESTATING POSITIONS STEP 6 entry compose cleanly, creating
   registry-site coherence for the body generation phase? (V-04)
5. Does full synthesis -- all three single axes plus STEP 6 in RESTATING POSITIONS -- produce
   a maximally anchored prompt where every generation site independently carries consequence-
   form? (V-05)

100.0. New pattern: consequence-form at an unregistered body-generation site. Gap exposed:
registry coverage does not extend to STEP 6. Candidate C-30 introduced; C-33 gap signal
