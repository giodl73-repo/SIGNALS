## V-01 -- Single Axis: Lifecycle Emphasis (Consequence-Form at STEP 5 TABLE CHECK)

**Axis:** Lifecycle emphasis -- STEP 5 TABLE CHECK gains consequence-form on its C-20 check
line. In R9 V-05, the check reads: "Compliance Contract C-20: every ## headline will carry
*(VM: VM-xxx-P#)* inline: Y / N" -- a yes/no compliance assertion without the criterion-level
failure declaration. V-01 upgrades this to: "Compliance Contract C-20: every ## headline will
carry *(VM: VM-xxx-P#)* inline -- an output with any vocabulary ID on a subline fails C-20
regardless of other compliance: Y / N". STEP 5 is the structural verification gate positioned
between planning (STEP 3B, VOCABULARY PRE-FLIGHT GATE) and body generation (STEP 6). Adding
CF here creates a pre-body enforcement checkpoint.

**Hypothesis:** Pure additive change. RESTATING POSITIONS is unchanged from R9 V-05 (five
entries: STEP 3B, VOCABULARY PRE-FLIGHT GATE, STEP 4, STEP 6, VERIFICATION MANIFEST). STEP 5
carries consequence-form but is NOT registered in RESTATING POSITIONS. Under v10, predicted
composite: 100.0. New pattern: consequence-form at an unregistered pre-body verification site.
Gap exposed: registry coverage does not extend to STEP 5. Candidate C-34 introduced; C-37 gap
signal exposed (STEP 5 enforces but is not registered).
