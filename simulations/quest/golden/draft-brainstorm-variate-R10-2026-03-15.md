## V-02 — Evidence Ledger Declaration

**Variation axis:** Evidence ledger declaration — Phase 1 declares both the challenge framework
AND a ledger blueprint table that maps annotation obligations to specific phases and names the
column, notation format, and carryforward target for each. Every phase inherits an explicit
annotation obligation before it begins.
**Hypothesis:** Declaring the ledger blueprint upfront converts annotation from a post-hoc
checklist concern into a pre-committed structural obligation. A phase cannot exit without
satisfying its ledger row. This removes the failure mode where the cluster T-Evidence column
appears but uses unstructured notation, or where Phase 7 does not carry Ph6 Pre-Score forward
— both become ledger violations visible at the phase boundary rather than at final verify.
