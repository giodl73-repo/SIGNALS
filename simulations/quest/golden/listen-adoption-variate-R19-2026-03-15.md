## V-01 — Single Axis: Lifecycle Emphasis (Protected Behavior in CORRECTION BLOCK Labels)

**Variation axis:** Lifecycle emphasis — when Gate H fires a per-I-0X CORRECTION BLOCK (C-52), the block label includes both the I-0X ID and the Protected behavior declared for that row in SECTION A's DOWNSTREAM CITATION CONTRACT. The correction label takes the form: `CORRECTION BLOCK -- I-03 CITATION CONTRACT VIOLATION -- Protected behavior: [the Early Majority incumbent habit this citation was meant to keep accountable]`. The Protected behavior field in the label is sourced directly from the C-54 contract column, not re-derived from phase bodies.

**Hypothesis:** C-52 makes correction labels self-identifying at the contract-row level: you can read `CORRECTION BLOCK -- I-03` and know which row failed without cross-referencing SECTION A. But the label still requires reading SECTION A's Protected behavior column to know what displacement stake was left unaddressed. Protected behavior in the label closes this: the correction is now self-describing at the displacement-stake level. A CORRECTION BLOCK whose label names I-03 AND its Protected behavior communicates not just "this contract row failed" but "this specific incumbent workflow escaped citation coverage." The correction record becomes a displacement-accountability record, not just a citation-coverage audit record. This is the correction-label complement of C-54: C-54 commits displacement stakes at contract generation; V-01's axis carries those stakes into every repair event.
