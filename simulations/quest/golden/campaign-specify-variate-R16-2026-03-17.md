the Citation Audit as downstream verifier of the STABILITY CITATION RECORD.

verifier of those costs. The inertia cost chain is the only content-producing
chain without a forward-pointing notice. V-01 adds STEP 0A FORWARD AUDIT
NOTICE immediately after the three inertia cost definitions in Step 0a, before
GATE 1, naming the STEP 0A INERTIA BINDING block in the COMPLETION INDEX Phase
Gate Audit as the downstream verifier and prohibiting alteration between Step 0a
and the audit. The hypothesis: adding the notice completes the forward-notice
position of the inertia cost chain (define → FORWARD AUDIT NOTICE → anchor →
gate → backward-bind), matching the structure of both the citation chain and
the voice contract chain, and creates a v17 excellence pattern. Predicted:
45/45 + v17 watch on STEP 0A FORWARD AUDIT NOTICE.
