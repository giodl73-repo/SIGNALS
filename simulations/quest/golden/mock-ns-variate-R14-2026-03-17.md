# mock-ns -- Round 14 Variations (rubric v14, C-38/C-39 targets)

Rubric: v14 (C-01 through C-39, 5 essential / 3 recommended / 31 aspirational, max 142).

New criteria in v14 (extracted from R13 scoring):

- **C-38** -- Expletive-subject it-cleft: "It is this step that first emits the TOPICS.md
  status line." Expletive `It` is formal subject; `this step` is cleft-focus NP; emission
  is in the restrictive relative clause `that first emits...`. C-29 does not fire (expletive
  is not a step-derived nominalization). C-35 does not fire (artifact is not main-clause
  subject). C-36 does not fire (main predicate is copula, not ordering verb). Failure chain:
  C-25, C-28, C-38 (-6 pts).

- **C-39** -- Wh-pseudo-cleft: "What this step does first is emit the TOPICS.md status line."
  Wh-action nominalization `What this step does first` is grammatical subject; emission
  is predicate complement after copula `is`. C-29 fires via substitution test: `It` = the
  wh-nominalization, not the step. Failure chain: C-25, C-28, C-29, C-39 (-8 pts).

Confirmed scores under v14 from R13:
  R13 V-01 ("It is this step that first emits..."): 136/142 -- fails C-25, C-28, C-38
  R13 V-02 ("What this step does first is emit..."): 134/142 -- fails C-25, C-28, C-29, C-39
  R13 V-03 ("This step runs first; it emits..."): 138/142 -- fails C-25, C-28 only
