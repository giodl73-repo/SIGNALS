#### Inertia Baseline: What This Campaign Replaces

An unstructured investigation has six characteristic failure modes. Each maps to exactly one governing
rule. This table is a preamble fixture -- it does not change as evidence accumulates.

| Antipattern | What It Looks Like | Governing Rule | Detection Point | Consequence |
|-------------|--------------------|-----------|----|-------------|

| **Attribution collapse** | Claims cite "the evidence" without naming the stage or source that produced them | ATTRIBUTION | Every stage POST | Non-compliant finding; stage-level ATTRIBUTION fails |
| **Uniform confidence** | All findings rated identically (all Medium, all High); no calibration against actual evidence strength | CALIBRATION | S4 POST, S5 POST | CALIBRATION fails; recalibration required before exit |
