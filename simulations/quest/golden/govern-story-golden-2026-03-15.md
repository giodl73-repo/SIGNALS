## What Made It Golden

**1. Dual-phase C-48 enforcement eliminates both classification failure modes.**
V-01 through V-03 placed the taxonomy enforcement in AUTHOR phase only -- where prose-frame pressure biases toward uniform classification. V-04 moved classification to EVALUATOR E-5 (pre-composition), which prevents AUTHOR drift. V-05 stacks both: EVALUATOR path diversity check before sealing E-5, then AUTHOR differentiation audit as a named C-46-form label. Neither phase alone catches the other's failure mode; both together close the loop.

**2. Cross-phase correction loop with named destination (C-51).**
Every other variation with a recovery instruction either stays local (V-02: "state explicitly why") or has no recovery at all (V-01, V-03). V-05's differentiation audit ends with "Return to E-5 and revise before proceeding" -- the only instruction that names the classification source as the correction target. A model that generated uniform tags in AUTHOR phase is sent back to the analytical phase where the error originated, not patched in place.

**3. Auto-transfer with verbatim fidelity eliminates AUTHOR discretion (C-50).**
Beat 4 reads: "Auto-transfer only from E-5 UNRESOLVED rows. Copy each UNRESOLVED row's finding and its pre-classified Resolution path tag verbatim. No new items, no omissions, no tag changes." The tags are frozen at E-5 and copied, not re-derived. AUTHOR cannot reclassify during composition.

**4. Axiom grammar for C-38/C-39 throughout.**
The VECTOR-STATUS AXIOM uses constitutive framing ("IS the E-8.5 re-derivation event") rather than causal framing ("triggers E-8.5"). This distinction is what satisfies C-38 and C-39 -- the act of writing the declaration is identical to the analytical event, not a precondition for it. All downstream references (Mid-Draft paths, E-8 compliance record, Beat 4.5 annotation) inherit this framing.

**5. Simplification preserved all 14 rubric-critical mechanisms at 20% word reduction.**
The QU5 simplification pass removed 444 words of documentation noise (restated axioms, illustrative examples, meta-commentary) while keeping every execution path, format field, gate label, and recovery instruction intact. The simplified body is shorter and more readable without losing a single pass criterion from C-35 through C-51.
