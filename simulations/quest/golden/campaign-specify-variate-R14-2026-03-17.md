Five complete, runnable skill body variations targeting the v14 rubric.
Single-axis first (V-01, V-02, V-03), then combined (V-04, V-05).

**R13 diagnosis driving R14 axis choices:**

R13 V-05 is the first 39/39 ceiling under v14. It carries two named novelties
above the R12 V-05 baseline (C-46 and C-47), and two unnamed structural blocks
that are v15 candidates:

- **C-46** — IMPERATIVE REGISTER: all instructional body text uses imperative
  mode throughout. First appeared in R13 V-01. Stable in V-02 through V-05.

- **C-47** — GATE CHAIN REQUIREMENT: named labeled requirement statement in
  Phase 0 explicitly declaring the four-step gate chain. First appeared in R13
  V-05.

- **STEP 0A INERTIA ANCHOR** (R13 V-02, unnamed in v14): named block at Phase 2
  entry, before any proposal option, requiring verbatim copy of all three Step 0a
  inertia costs. Structural parallel to STEP 0C CONTRACT ANCHOR at Phase 3 entry.
  Present in R13 V-02 through V-05. No gate after it in any R13 variation.

- **STEP 0B CITATION BINDING** (R13 V-03, unnamed in v14): named source-binding
  block in the Citation Audit, explicitly naming Step 0b as the authoritative
  source for the pasted STABILITY CITATION RECORD. Structural parallel to
  STEP 0C SOURCE BINDING in the Voice Compliance Audit. Present in R13 V-03
  through V-05.

The v14 ceiling carries the inertia anchor chain and citation audit binding, but
neither has a complete four-position structure:

- Voice contract chain (complete): define (Step 0c + FORWARD AUDIT NOTICE) →
  anchor before use (STEP 0C CONTRACT ANCHOR at Phase 3) → gate at anchor
  (ANCHOR GATE) → backward bind at audit (STEP 0C SOURCE BINDING)

- Citation chain (two of four): define (Step 0b) → [no anchor before Defeating
  Do Nothing] → requirement (CITATION FORM REQUIREMENT) → backward bind at
  audit (STEP 0B CITATION BINDING)

- Inertia cost chain (one of four): define (Step 0a) → anchor before use
  (STEP 0A INERTIA ANCHOR at Phase 2 entry) → [no gate after anchor] →
  [no backward bind at audit]

- Gate chain (two of four): define (Phase 0 steps + GATE CHAIN REQUIREMENT) →
  [no anchor] → [no gate] → [no backward bind at audit; Phase Gate Audit records
  but does not source-bind]

R14 probes three new axes to complete missing positions in the citation, inertia,
and gate chains.

## V-01 — Axis: INERTIA ANCHOR GATE after STEP 0A INERTIA ANCHOR

to STEP 0C CONTRACT ANCHOR at Phase 3 entry). The STEP 0C CONTRACT ANCHOR has
a gate (ANCHOR GATE, C-45) that blocks pitch writing until the anchor is complete.

is required but nothing explicitly prohibits options writing before the anchor is
filled in. V-01 adds an INERTIA ANCHOR GATE immediately after the STEP 0A INERTIA

three inertia costs are present in the anchor block. The hypothesis: an inertia
anchor gate (parallel to ANCHOR GATE for voice contracts) creates a v15 excellence
pattern. The inertia cost chain becomes: define → anchor → GATE → options. All
C-46, C-47, and C-09 through C-45 preserved. Predicted: 39/39 + v15 watch.
