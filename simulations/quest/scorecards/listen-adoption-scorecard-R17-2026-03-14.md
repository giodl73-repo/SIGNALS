### Excellence Signals from V-05

**Pattern 1 — Phase-open displacement inheritance closes the phase-boundary gap.**
C-45 gave six exit-state anchors. V-01/V-04/V-05's `Displacement state at phase entry:` field adds six entry-state anchors, forming full `(entry → body → exit)` tuples. Discontinuity is architecturally visible at both sides of every phase boundary, not only at close. The phase body no longer operates between two exit-state anchors held in context — it inherits state at its own opening.

**Pattern 2 — Terminal section as self-sufficient contract certificate.**
V-02/V-04/V-05's Citation Contract Completion Record in SECTION K collapses the SECTION H cross-reference requirement. C-30 already proved audit execution; this proves contract satisfaction without the reader leaving SECTION K. The terminal section now proves two independent things: that the audit ran (per-gate stamps) and that the contract was satisfied (19-row Y/N completion record).

**Pattern 3 — Source-declared audit form prevents gate-level structural deviation.**
V-03/V-04/V-05's `Gate H Status:` column in SECTION A pre-declares the exact rows Gate H must fill. Gate H cannot abbreviate, reorder, or collapse rows without violating the pre-declared slot contract. This is the audit-form analogue of C-29's answer-form elevation: the audit structure is encoded at the contract source (SECTION A generation time), not generated at the audit destination (SECTION H execution time).
