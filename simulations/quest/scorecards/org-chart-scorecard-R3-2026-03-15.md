## Excellence Signals

**From V-04 (first to reach 100.0):**

1. **Typed evolution table is orthogonal to the six R2 criteria.** The Org Evolution Roadmap section with Trigger/Structural Change/Type columns adds C-09 without any disruption to C-11–C-16. The table format forces concreteness (trigger must name a threshold; structural change must name what changes). No interaction effect observed even when combined with anti-pattern section.

2. **Named anti-pattern table with "Why It Applies Here" column is similarly orthogonal.** Anti-Pattern Watch reliably produces C-10 credit because the column heading itself enforces domain specificity. The section list of candidate anti-pattern names (committee capture, shiproom without a DRI) prevents blank cells. Appending this to the V-04/R2 foundation does not disturb decision-rights or inertia structure.

3. **Simultaneous 8-aspirational targeting via section addition does not overload the prompt.** The two new sections are structurally isolated (after Committee Charters) and do not touch the inertia or headcount blocks that carry C-11–C-16. The V-04/R3 hypothesis (no interaction effects when both sections appended) is confirmed.

**From V-05 (confirms V-04 finding, adds register evidence):**

4. **Counter-examples are the load-bearing mechanism, not imperative register.** Each criterion that V-04 enforces with "DO NOT" is matched in V-05 with "does not name a mechanism / destination / valid trigger." These negations produce equivalent rubric compliance. The finding is actionable: future skill authors can choose register freely provided each positive requirement is paired with a concrete negative example.

**From V-03 (regression finding):**

5. **C-11 + C-14 require mechanism-typed reasons inside the Case for Staying Flat, not adjacent to it.** The 4-part structure (separating How We Coordinate Today as its own sub-section) moves mechanism-typing out of the steelman, causing C-11 to fail the "at least two reasons" test and C-14 to auto-fail. The V-04/R2 approach — embedding numbered mechanism-typed reasons inside "Case for Staying Flat" — is more reliable for both criteria jointly. The 4-part structure is superior for C-15 reliability (explicit inventory label) but inferior for C-11/C-14.
