### Excellence Signals (from top-scoring variations)

1. **Four-part causal chain in AMEND (V-10):** Requiring Intervention / Root cause addressed / Causal mechanism / Expected signal at T=6mo as named subfields is the most reliable structural closure for C-13. When the chain is labeled with explicit sub-parts, the model cannot conflate what the intervention does with why it works. Referencing Step 4(a) and Step 4(b) by name prevents the mitigation from floating free of the kill-barrier analysis.

2. **Temporal grid makes C-10 structurally required (V-07):** Placing inertia scores in three time-horizon columns means T=6mo and T=18mo cannot be copies of T=0 by construction -- the format forces time-dependency reasoning. Static snapshot formats can satisfy every other field without ever producing a time-dependent statement.

3. **Competitive inventory with Dimension/Advantage/Durability and explicit familiarity disqualifier (V-08):** The three-field structure per competitive strength -- with "familiarity is not durability" as an explicit disqualifier -- is the only mechanism that fully closes C-11. Structural durability is analytically distinct from identifying a kill barrier; the competitive inventory enforces that distinction at the field level.

4. **Baseline table named social proof threshold (V-07):** Moving the social proof requirement into a table column labeled "not binary Y/N" with format examples is a lighter structural mechanism for C-12 than a full ladder. The table cell cannot hold "needs peers" without visibly failing the format requirement -- the constraint is self-enforcing.

5. **C-09 remains isolated:** V-06 is the only variation that passes C-09. No variation achieves both C-09 (methodology declared) and C-10 (temporal sensitivity) simultaneously. A variation stacking V-06's Phase 1 methodology declaration + V-07's temporal grid + V-10's four-part causal chain would target C-09 + C-10 + C-12 + C-13 = 40 aspirational points without C-11.
