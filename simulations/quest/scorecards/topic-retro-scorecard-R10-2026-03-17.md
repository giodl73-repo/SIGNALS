### Excellence Signals from V-05

Three mechanisms made V-05 the ceiling variation:

**1. Guard-at-SEAL pattern** — Precision anti-failure checks embedded within SEAL blocks go beyond structural presence. The Phase 7 SEAL check `"no '[placeholder]' strings remain"` is not just a format check — it is an active regression guard that prevents structurally-valid but semantically-empty recommendations from passing the seal.

**2. Content-type contract pattern** — Naming the content *type* of each column (PRIOR BELIEF / DISCOVERY) as a structural pre-condition rather than a style suggestion. This isolates semantic drift: a cell that answers WHY cannot satisfy a WHAT requirement by accident, regardless of phrasing.

**3. Copy-not-compute guard** — Explicit prohibition on recomputing a value that has an upstream authoritative source (`"copy Phase 4 TOTAL directly — do not recompute"`). This prevents the Phase 8 calibration table from drifting from Phase 6 due to rounding or arithmetic variance.
