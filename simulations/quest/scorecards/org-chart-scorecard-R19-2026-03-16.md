### Excellence Signals from V-05

**Signal 1: Named vocabulary contract as pre-section standalone block**
V-05 introduces RATIONALE-FORM-CONTRACT between the ROLES gate and the INERTIA ASSESSMENT section — parallel to how VIOLATION-LABEL-CONTRACT is a named block separate from the correspondence section instructions. The contract is independently scannable: a reviewer checking C-50 can locate it by name rather than parsing the multi-line INERTIA ASSESSMENT instruction body. This is the first use of a pre-section vocabulary contract governing rationale form (as opposed to label form or gate artifact form).

**Signal 2: Dual-site constraint enforcement — standalone contract + inline section reference**
V-05 enforces C-50 at two structural sites: the standalone RATIONALE-FORM-CONTRACT block (naming STEELMAN FIRST under PROHIBITED forms) and the inline INERTIA ASSESSMENT reference ("DO NOT use structural-ordering devices (e.g., STEELMAN FIRST)"). This dual-site pattern matches the triple-site principle (C-16) and raises the detectability of the prohibition beyond any single-site form. V-04 has only the single inline site; V-05 adds the contract block as a second scan surface.
