## Excellence Signals from V-05

**Signal 1 — ROLE-NAME LOCK self-documenting activation layer**

V-05's ROLE-NAME LOCK block explicitly documents which sites carry which enforcement granularity:
> "Sites 2 and 5 carry per-cell row-by-row directives in their section instructions. Site 3 carries per-role-name directives in the Membership and Decides field instructions. These are the activation layer for this lock at those sites."

This converts the ROLE-NAME LOCK from a pure enumeration into a visible enforcement hierarchy map. The lock itself cross-references the per-granularity mechanisms, making the four-level hierarchy (document declaration → section CHECK → column/field instruction → row/name per-item verify) inspectable from a single block without reading downstream section instructions.

**Signal 2 — BEFORE-PRODUCE explicitly activates the embedded structural CHECK**

V-05's BEFORE PRODUCING THE REBUTTAL directive does not just name the field labels — it explicitly says:
> "The embedded CHECK at ROLE UNDER PRESSURE fires before the first field is written — do not skip it."

This creates a two-step temporal activation chain: (1) re-read the four-field form definition and confirm all four labels are ready, then (2) the embedded CHECK fires before field population begins. V-04's equivalent directive names the field order but does not activate the embedded CHECK. The absence of this bridge in V-04 leaves a gap: the BEFORE-PRODUCE re-read and the form-embedded CHECK are two separate mechanisms that do not reference each other, so a prompt that satisfies "re-read the labels" could proceed to fill fields without triggering the CHECK. V-05 closes this by making the CHECK activation an explicit instruction within the BEFORE-PRODUCE step itself.
