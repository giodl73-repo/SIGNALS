### Excellence Signals

**R15 achieved its design target**: all 5 variations score 100.00 by universalizing C-32 and C-33. The ceiling is flat at 100.00. The next ceiling opportunity requires a new pattern not captured in C-01–C-33.

Three new structural patterns appear exclusively in V-05 that are candidates for v13 criteria:

**1. Falsifiability Contract on Team Insight (Seed X)**
V-05 §5.3 appends two lines after the insight sentence: `[holds if: {specific observable condition}]` / `[falsified by: {specific observable condition}]`. Both conditions must name a specific topic, contributor, or metric threshold — vague conditions fail. This goes beyond C-13 (structural form), C-25 (novelty gate), and Seed L (projection test) by making the insight directly falsifiable by a named future observable state. No current criterion tests this dimension.

**2. Cross-Role Discrepancy Check (Seed I — Inspector §3.3)**
V-05 runs a structured comparison of the Assessor's inertia flag assignments against the Inspector's Health Phase findings — a Discrepancy Table that catches cases where the Assessor raised a flag that the Health Phase data contradicts (e.g., `stuck-at-first` flagged but Signal Depth EARNED). Retractions flow downstream: the Strategist's Pre-Write Check excludes both echo-retracted and discrepancy-retracted flags. C-33 tests flag/achievement logical coexistence; this tests flag/health-data empirical consistency — orthogonal.

**3. 2D Action Matrix (Round × Tier)**
V-05 §4.3 groups actions by both round (gap distance: Round 1/2/3) AND severity tier (CRITICAL/WARNING/ADVANCING) within each round. C-08 tests gap prioritization; C-26/C-29 test structure and gates. No current criterion tests whether the action output is organized as a two-dimensional matrix where both temporal horizon and topic severity are simultaneously encoded per row.
