## Excellence Signals from V-05

**1. C-67 gate-blocking via gate checklist item**
Adding TABLE F Analytical Purpose compliance as gate checklist item [7] elevates C-67 from "schema aspiration" to "gate condition." Combined with the PHASE OUTPUTS footer confirming "Analytical Purpose form (C-67) on all rows: [YES/NO]", omission becomes detectable in three independent locations: schema declaration, gate checklist failure, and footer flag.

**2. Dual verification paths for all three new criteria**
The combination of maximum preamble density (declares what will be produced) and phase footer blocks (confirms what was produced) means C-67, C-68, and C-69 are each verifiable without reading the phase body — preamble is sufficient for structural check, footer is sufficient for count/compliance check.

**3. Footer row-count confirmation as second C-68/C-69 verification path**
GROUP A PHASE OUTPUTS explicitly states COVERAGE ATTESTATION row count with MET/NOT MET status (C-68); GROUP B PHASE OUTPUTS states TABLE H row count vs pre-production assertion with match status (C-69). A scorer can verify C-68 compliance from the footer alone without reading the gate checklist.
