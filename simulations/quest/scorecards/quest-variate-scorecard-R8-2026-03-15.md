### Excellence Signals from V-05 (Top Combination)

**E-01: Enforcement-link sub-section in contract artifacts**
V-05's STRUCTURAL KEY CONTRACT contains a dedicated ENFORCEMENT: block that names the Structural Key Uniformity Auditor role as its enforcement mechanism, states that the two artifacts "form a protocol chain," and asserts that "neither is complete without the other." This converts a standalone declaration artifact into the authoritative source half of a two-artifact compliance system — the contract becomes incomplete without its named verifier. Goes beyond C-29 (which links VARIATION MAP → AXIS-FREEZE) by generalizing the source-declaration pattern to any contract + auditor artifact pair.

**E-02: Mutual-incompleteness assertion between paired artifacts**
Both the STRUCTURAL KEY CONTRACT and the Structural Key Uniformity Auditor role explicitly reference each other: the contract names the auditor as its enforcement gate; the auditor "reads the required-key list from the STRUCTURAL KEY CONTRACT." The explicit "neither is complete without the other" statement makes structural independence of either artifact a detectable protocol failure. C-29 achieves one-directional read-source linking; E-02 achieves bidirectional co-dependence declaration, making the absence of either artifact falsifiable against the other.
