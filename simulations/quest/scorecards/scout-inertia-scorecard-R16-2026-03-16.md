## Excellence Signals

**From V-01 (reference form):** The SECTION N scaffold with `SECTION 2 COMPLETION GATE` is the cleanest demonstration that all 30 advanced criteria can coexist without structural collision. Every implication chain terminates cleanly.

**From V-04 (FAILURE VECTORS):** The directional locus metaphor (VECTORS = propagation pathways) provides a higher-density heading subtitle than spatial (SURFACE) or discrete-point (POINTS) metaphors: it encodes the FM→Actor and FM→Trigger bridge architecture directly in the noun, making the A-38 form anticipate the A-12 bridge structure semantically.

**From V-02 (STRUCTURAL FAULTS):** Demonstrates that TYPE=structural accepts multiple LOCUS nouns (WEAKNESSES, FAULTS), confirming that A-38 is a compound-noun form criterion, not a specific-phrase criterion. The engineering register of FAULTS is slightly more precise than WEAKNESSES.

**From V-05 (COLLAPSE POINTS):** Demonstrates TYPE severity gradient: FAILURE POINTS (partial failure) → COLLAPSE POINTS (total breakdown). Both satisfy A-38. The TYPE component can carry severity encoding without violating the compound-noun form.

**From V-03 (EACH + singular ARTIFACT):** Confirms that A-39 quantifier satisfaction is independent of plural/singular noun form: EACH BRIDGE ARTIFACT (singular) is equivalent to ALL/BOTH BRIDGE ARTIFACTS (plural) for cardinality encoding purposes.
