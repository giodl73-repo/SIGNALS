### Structural Finding: C-07 as the R8 Design Cost

All five variations score 10 points below R7 equivalents due to the set-level C-07 FAIL. This is not an execution error — it is the structural cost of R8's measurement design. Placing three variations (V-02, V-03, V-04) within the same axis family (inertia framing density gradient) achieves the C-28 gradient measurement but reduces canonical axis coverage to 2 of 6. The trade-off is: **measurement precision over axis breadth.**

This is the first round where the variate set's experimental focus causes a set-level recommended-criterion failure. In all prior rounds, the 4+ ablation variations targeted distinct criteria (C-27, C-25, C-26, C-24 in R7), naturally distributing axis coverage. R8's gradient design concentrates all ablation power in one axis — gaining scientific depth but losing C-07.
