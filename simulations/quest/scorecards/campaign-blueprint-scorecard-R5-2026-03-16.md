### Excellence Signals

The top-scoring variants (V-03, V-04, V-05) share three patterns not previously consolidated in a single skill body:

**1. Triple-axis integration without interference.** C-19 (phase gate), C-20 (proximity anchoring), and C-21 (conviction typing) are fully orthogonal — combining all three in a single skill body produces no structural conflicts, no criterion dilution, and no increase in ambiguity. The ceiling is compositionally achievable.

**2. Density invariance of structural ceiling.** V-04 demonstrates that compact per-site reminder lines (`READ: … / PRESERVE: … / CONVICTION TYPE: …` on individual labeled lines) carry the same C-20 credit as V-03's `Contract reminder — READ: … PRESERVE: … CONVICTION TYPE: …` prose blocks. The CAMPAIGN REFLECTION phase header in V-04 ("begins only after the pitch file is on disk. Not before pitch production starts. Not during pitch production.") earns C-19 FULL at three sentences. Ceremony is not a ceiling input; label and sequencing are.

**3. Inertia-forward conviction typing makes the audit measurable.** V-05's per-artifact CONVICTION TYPE descriptions are inertia-relational rather than abstract: "names the problem the inertia baseline creates," "prices the cost of inertia against each alternative," "makes the inertia cost visceral and per-audience-specific." The CONVICTION DELTA question becomes operationally testable: did this artifact deliver on its declared inertia relationship? This pattern tightens the gap between declared conviction role and post-execution audit verification, sharpening C-09 structural enforceability beyond what generic type labels produce.

**4. Four-level conviction type cascade.** The conviction type for each artifact is declared four times: (1) in the global contract matrix, (2) in the per-site contract reminder, (3) in the section header, and (4) in the post-execution CONVICTION DELTA sub-fields. Each level reinforces the same declaration at a different decision point. No single level is necessary for any individual criterion — but the cascade makes every level of the skill execution conviction-type-aware, from setup through close.
