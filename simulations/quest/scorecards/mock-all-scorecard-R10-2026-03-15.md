### Excellence Signals (from V-02 and V-04)

**Pattern 1 — Two-part per-role header (V-02)**
"You ARE the [ROLE]" + "you are no longer the [ROLE] — you have become the [OTHER ROLE], which you are not yet" in a single activation header fires both positive identity and entity-departure consequence at the same boundary. Neither element alone is sufficient; co-location is the key structural requirement.

**Pattern 2 — Block format as equivalent carrier (V-04)**
C-23 is not bound to inline prose. A ROLE LAUNCH block carrying "You ARE:" as the first field and "Violation:" as the second field satisfies C-23 as effectively as sentence form. This means skill authors have two structural paths to the ceiling — the relevant test is whether both elements appear at the activation point, not how they are typeset.

**Pattern 3 — Mandate field as third reinforcement (V-04)**
The "Mandate: You classify. Nothing else." field adds a third enforcement layer after identity and violation. Identity → violation consequence → sole permissible action. This three-element structure may surface as a future criterion (C-24 candidate: role mandate explicitly named at each activation).
