## Excellence Signals from V-01

1. **Gap-count ceiling with floor**: "Minimum: one gap. Maximum: three gaps" forces prioritization and prevents retrospective sprawl. Neither too thin nor too diffuse.

2. **AMEND omission footer**: "If AMEND is set, include only signals in the specified type or window. Note omissions at table bottom." Makes scope restriction transparent without losing traceability.

3. **Named-field structure in Echo section**: Three explicit sub-fields (Echo / Why it wasn't predicted / Systemic pattern) prevent the Echo from being written as a prose paragraph that conflates observation, causation, and pattern. Each field requires a distinct cognitive operation.

4. **Accuracy ratio enforced at table close, not at section end**: Placing "Accuracy: N correct / M total = X%" immediately after the table anchors it to the evidence rather than burying it in a summary paragraph.

5. **PARTIAL verdict explicitly disambiguated**: "PARTIAL counts as wrong for accuracy scoring" removes the escape valve that allows a model to inflate accuracy by soft-crediting weak predictions.
