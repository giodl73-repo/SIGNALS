## Excellence Signals

Patterns from the top-scoring variation (V-04, 97.5) that drove the score above V-03/V-05:

**1. Interrogative phrasing creates obligation, not option.**
V-04 frames every phase as questions the model must answer before proceeding. "Do not proceed past a phase until every question has a concrete answer." This is stronger than imperative phrasing because questions require specific answers — imperatives can be satisfied with vague prose. The model cannot write "organize sources" and move on; it must answer "Which sources are FOUNDATIONAL?"

**2. Explicit anti-placeholder rule with a recovery instruction.**
"No placeholder entries. If a cell is unknown, note it and search again." V-02 has a similar rule ("Do not use 'Author et al.' as a placeholder"). Both beat the baseline and other variations on C-07. The recovery instruction ("search again") is the critical addition — it closes the escape hatch of leaving unknown cells blank.

**3. Mandatory "NONE FOUND" scaffolding for empty tiers.**
"Every tier must have at least one answer. If a tier has no entries, write 'NONE FOUND — explain why and what additional search is needed.'" This prevents silent omission — the model cannot skip a tier, it must explicitly acknowledge the gap. No other variation has this exact construction (V-03 has per-tier minimum instructions; V-05 has a note that empty FOUNDATIONAL means review is incomplete — but neither forces a "NONE FOUND" acknowledgment with explanation).
