### Excellence Signals — V-01

**What made V-01 the top scorer:**

1. **Named pre-stage gate (GATE S)**: Elevating scout loading to a named STAGE 0 with an explicit gate that must be passed before hypothesis formation is structurally different from all other variations. It removes the possibility of retrospective grounding — the model cannot write the hypothesis before the scout record is committed. This is a new structural pattern beyond C-07 ("explicit handoff"), which only requires the handoff to exist, not to *gate* the next stage.

2. **Required frontmatter anchor field in hypothesis artifact**: Mandating a `scout-anchor` slug in the hypothesis output frontmatter creates a durable, traceable link from STAGE 0 to STAGE 1. The link is in the artifact itself, not in the instructions. This enforces traceability structurally rather than instructionally — the artifact cannot be used downstream without the anchor.

3. **Per-artifact path enforcement of topic prefix**: Specifying `` `{topic}-` in every path `` (rather than "named at opening") was the only differentiator that earned C-05 PASS across V-01 and V-02. The three lowest-scoring variations all said "named at opening" and received partial credit. This is a small change with outsized scoring impact on an essential criterion.
