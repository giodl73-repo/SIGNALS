## Excellence Signals — V-01

**Signal 1 — IF-* forward mesh pre-declaration with fulfillment contract**

Role 0 does not just label failure modes — it pre-declares *which specific TC-2 and TC-3 entries will carry each label*. Role 1 has a contractual obligation, not a general annotation instruction. Every expected TC entry is either confirmed or ⚠-flagged. An orphaned IF-* label (declared but uncorroborated) becomes detectable at catalog-construction time — earlier than pathology detection can catch it. This is the difference between aspirational annotation and structural enforcement.

Why it outperformed: V-03's Mesh Completeness Check runs *after* cataloging and detects gaps post-hoc. V-01's fulfillment contract detects gaps *during* cataloging, giving Role 1 an inline accountability mechanism.

**Signal 2 — Prediction-callback loop as pathology evidence substrate**

Role 4 explicitly returns to Role 0's predictions before rendering verdicts: "which predictions were confirmed, which were unfulfilled?" This transforms pathology from pattern detection ("does the evidence match a storm signature?") to hypothesis verification ("did my pre-analysis claims hold?"). The callback produces richer evidence because the model closes a loop it was told to open — unfulfilled predictions become evidence of indeterminate or missed pathologies, not just absent data.

Why it outperformed: V-04 uses similar phrasing-register framing ("return to your claims") but does not mandate the explicit callback in the pathology section structure, leaving it as tone rather than contract.

**Differentiator over V-05 — Explicit verdict-first and 3-layer in role mandate vs. schema implication**

Both V-01 and V-05 achieve C-19 (three-layer remediation). V-01 achieves C-14 (specialist accountability chain for all five roles) and C-16 (verdict-first) by explicit mandate; V-05 achieves C-16 via schema implication but fails C-14 because BLOCK B–F carry no named roles. The role-sequence axis in V-01 distributes accountability across five named agents, while V-05's block-schema axis concentrates it on one named agent (Inertia Analyst) and leaves the rest anonymous.
