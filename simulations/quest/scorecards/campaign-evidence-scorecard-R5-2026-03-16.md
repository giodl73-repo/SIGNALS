## Excellence Signals (from V-05)

1. **Inertia position as decision-displacement baseline** — Declaring what the team would have believed without the investigation, then tracking per-finding whether it confirms or challenges that prior, makes the `**Decision readiness**` section actionable at a higher register than "Ready/Not ready": the brief records *what specifically shifted* and *what the prior assumption got wrong*. This adds structural depth to C-08 and C-09 simultaneously — gaps are now defined as "what the inertia position assumed but the evidence left unanswered," not just "open questions."

2. **Retrospective audit anchored against prospective declarations** — The RULE INVOCATION AUDIT TABLE in V-05 lists expected invocations derived from the preamble's inline annotations ("Expected (from rule annotations): CALIBRATION at 4, 5 | FALSIFICATION at 3, 5 | ..."). This creates a self-verifying loop: the prospective commitment sets the expected pattern; the audit detects deviations against it. Without the prior commitment to anchor against, the audit is a checklist; with it, the audit is a contract enforcement.
