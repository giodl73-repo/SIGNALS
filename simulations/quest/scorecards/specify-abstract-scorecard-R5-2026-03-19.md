### Excellence Signals from V-05 (Top Variation)

**E-01 — Structured revision directive parallelism**
V-05 (via V-01) mirrors Phase 5 amendment format at Phase 2c. The parallel structure (Boundary / Deficient section / Deficiency / Before / After / Re-verify) creates consistent before/after evidence at two lifecycle positions. This reduces cognitive load when the model interprets the revision task and produces verifiable evidence of coupling repair — the same mechanism that made Phase 5 amendments substantive over baseline drafting.

**E-02 — Bridge integration audit closes a structural gap**
V-05 (via V-02) adds Phase 3b as the only lifecycle verification point after merge. Phase 2b produces planned bridges; Phase 3 uses them as "merge glue" but has no verification. Phase 3b makes bridge integration an observable, verifiable claim. The criterion of "Y if semantic type preserved, N if replaced by stock connector" is falsifiable and specific — a model cannot nominally pass Phase 3b without genuinely incorporating the planned bridges.

**E-03 — Terminal self-diagnosis targets post-amendment residuals**
V-05 (via V-03) adds Amendment 5 as an unconstrained terminal sweep. The constraint "must not repeat a target already fully resolved by amendments 1-4" forces the model to look beyond Gap / Result / Implication / Prose — the four named categories. This creates the first mechanism that could catch systematic residuals: Background that is accurate but too generic, Claim that names a result but not a contribution, Method that names a technique but not a scope.

**E-04 — Three enforcement layers are additive and non-conflicting**
Phase 2c (pre-bridge coupling), Phase 3b (post-merge bridge fidelity), and Amendment 5 (post-amendment quality sweep) operate at distinct lifecycle positions with distinct failure modes. None subsumes another: coupling fixes semantic drift before bridges are composed; bridge audit fixes expression fidelity after merge; self-diagnosis fixes residual content weakness after targeted amendments. V-04 vs V-05 will test whether Amendment 5 adds incremental signal or whether its value is subsumed by the coupling and bridge layers.

**E-05 — Attention load at terminal phase is the binding constraint**
V-05 is the longest prompt of any round. The self-preamble names three enforcement layers explicitly, which may orient model attention — but Phase 5 occurs after the full prompt has been processed. If Amendment 5 self-diagnosis quality shows compression in execution, the finding is architectural: maximum-fidelity abstract authoring may require splitting the lifecycle across two prompts (draft-to-merge and amendment-to-final) rather than stacking all enforcement in one.
