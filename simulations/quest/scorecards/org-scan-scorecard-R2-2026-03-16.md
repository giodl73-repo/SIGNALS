## Excellence Signals from V-05

**What closed the remaining gap from R1 V-01 (98) to 100:**

1. **Per-section anti-fabrication fires at the decision point** — moving the constraint from preamble to each evidence-dependent section (2a, 2b, 2c, 3a, 3b) means it activates when the model is actively making a claim, not during setup where it is forgotten.

2. **5-path absolute floor eliminates absent-source vulnerability** — "at least one path per source" fails when many sources are ABSENT; "5 most specific paths found" is source-count-independent. R1 V-01 was PARTIAL on C-09 exactly because this case wasn't closed.

3. **Phase gate sentence is structurally lighter than a checklist but closes the gap** — V-04's 5-item checklist is mechanically heavy. V-05's single gate sentence ("write confirmation before Phase 2") achieves the same sequencing guarantee with lower prompt bulk.

**What V-04's explicit STOP checkpoint revealed:** Anti-fabrication (C-11) is not a free corollary of structural design — you must explicitly budget for it or it will be crowded out by other structural concerns. V-04 optimized hard gates (C-12) at the cost of C-11 entirely.
