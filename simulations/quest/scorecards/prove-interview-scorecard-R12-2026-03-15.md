## Excellence Signals (from V-02/V-05)

**1. Prohibition-first gate clauses** — Inverting the gate text to open with PROHIBITED before REQUIRED makes scope violations detectable by reading the gate alone, without inspecting adjacent phase content. This transforms the gate from a completion checklist into an active scope guard.

**2. PROFILE RELEVANCE column bridges setup framework to extraction** — Declaring tag vocabulary in the Phase 0A inertia profile (and at the gate level in V-05) propagates a named set forward to Phase 3 evidence items and Phase 4 INERTIA PROFILE ACCOUNTING. The column satisfies C-36 and enables Phase 4 to close by tag rather than by content recall — structural enforcement, not authorial memory.

**3. Two named structural sources in MOMENT citation** — Making the MOMENT template cite both the Phase 0C tier prediction (subject-specific expectation) AND the Phase 0C adjacency baseline (sequence-grounded contrast baseline) satisfies C-37 and makes the label's diagnostic value auditable against two distinct structural claims.

**V-03's bidirectional audit table** is an elegant standalone innovation (two-row table with upper/lower boundary as separate cells forces format completion), but it doesn't add v11 criterion coverage because no v11 criterion specifically captures that pattern. It points toward a future criterion.
