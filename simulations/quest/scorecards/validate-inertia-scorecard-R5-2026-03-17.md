### Excellence Signals from V-25

**Signal 1 -- Phase-zero methodology declaration as gating infrastructure** (C-09 + C-17 cascade):
V-25 places dimension weights and tier thresholds in Phase 1, before any persona exists. This makes C-17's per-score trace column structurally resolvable -- every Phase 4 trace has a declared threshold to cite. Without Phase 1, C-17 traces have nothing to reference, producing a cascade double-failure: C-09 and C-17 fail together across all four other variations. **Signal 2 -- Competitive strength inventory embedded in persona phase** (C-11 + C-15 upstream dependency):

Embedding the inventory in the persona phase means the structural durability property is named before it is needed, not invented on demand. Phase 5's kill barrier sub-part (2) can reference the Phase 2 durability property by name rather than constructing it from scratch.
