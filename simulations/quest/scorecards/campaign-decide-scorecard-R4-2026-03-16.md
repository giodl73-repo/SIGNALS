## Excellence Signals from V-04

1. **Arithmetic formula declaration for slot-count equality**: `SLOT COUNT = PHASE COUNT = N` stated as an integer equality in both the template preamble and the synthesis preamble makes C-17 self-certifying. A scorer cannot miscount because the constraint is stated, not implied.

2. **Numbered structural rules block at template head**: "FOUR STRUCTURAL RULES — enforced by template position, not by instruction compliance" opens the template and enumerates all enforcement mechanisms explicitly (register first, hypothesis first, FID citations, slot match). The analyst sees the full constraint set before executing any phase.

3. **Finding register as pre-campaign skeleton table**: The `## FINDING REGISTER` section is a complete table-of-contents for every finding across all phases, committed as document structure before Phase 0. The template then fills that skeleton rather than building it. This closes both C-15 (stable FIDs) and C-16 (pre-seeded infrastructure) simultaneously and resolves the R3 ambiguity without ambiguity.
