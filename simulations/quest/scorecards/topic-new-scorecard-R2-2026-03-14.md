## Excellence Signals (R2)

1. **Pervasive consequence framing** (V-05) — attaching "what breaks downstream" to every field rule and coverage requirement, not just priority vocabulary. C-12's logic generalizes: any constraint worth enforcing should carry its operational failure mode. V-05 applies this to namespace validity, item naming, owner assignment, and the Naming Reference section itself — no other variation does the last one.

2. **Stakeholder-risk-first sequencing** (V-04) — "who gets hurt if this design is wrong?" as the opening frame produces organically differentiated role assignments. When the model must ask who carries each risk, it naturally distributes across role types. The rationale template inherits this frame, making C-07 and C-08 structurally connected rather than independent checklist items.

3. **Structural compression proof** (V-03) — all 13 criteria satisfied in ~50 lines with no criterion degraded. The three R1 structural patterns transfer intact to extreme compression. This isolates structure as the reliability mechanism and rules out elaboration, repetition, and length as causal factors.
