### Excellence Signals from V-05 (Top Variation -- Full Convergence)

V-05 is the structural frontier variation. Where V-03 R10 proved that three enforcement loops form a "mesh" (C-27), V-05 proves that five enforcement loops -- each targeting a structurally independent domain -- remain coherent. The distinguishing properties:

**1. Enforcement-loop self-annotation.** VIOLATION-12 and VIOLATION-13 in V-05 carry explicit loop numbering: "Enforcement loop 4 of 5" and "Enforcement loop 5 of 5." This metadata makes the mesh density legible to a reviewer without counting VIOLATION-NN entries manually. It also distinguishes "three loops targeting named criteria (C-27 pass condition)" from "five loops targeting five distinct structural domains (the C-28 candidate property)."

**2. Structural domain coverage across pre-stage, per-stage, artifact-level, and evidence-level concerns.** The five enforcement loops in V-05 span:
- Pre-stage inertia tracking (VIOLATION-09, VIOLATION-10)
- Per-stage exit certification (VIOLATION-11)
- Document-level execution sequencing (VIOLATION-12 -- new)
- Artifact-level provenance (VIOLATION-13 -- new)
This is the broadest structural coverage in the R11 series.

**3. Evidence Quality Summary in synthesis.** V-03, V-04, V-05 introduce this section as a first-class synthesis artifact: a tabular count of findings by DOCUMENTED / OBSERVED / INFERRED, with HIGH count and unresolved HIGH INFERRED count. The section makes evidence quality independently measurable at the document level -- similar to how the Inertia Pressure Summary makes displacement measurable.

**4. VIOLATION-15 as a second evidence gate.** V-03 gates HIGH INFERRED findings individually (VIOLATION-12). V-05's VIOLATION-15 gates the *GO signal* itself: a GO issued with any unresolved HIGH INFERRED findings in the ledger is a structural violation. This two-tier evidence gating pattern (per-finding gate + verdict gate) is parallel to how C-11 (per-stage exit condition) and C-16 (synthesis residual inventory) create two-tier phase gate visibility.
