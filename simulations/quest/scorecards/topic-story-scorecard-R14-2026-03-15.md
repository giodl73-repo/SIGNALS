## Excellence Signals from Top Variation (V-01)

V-01 scores above V-02 solely because of display completeness. The structural excellence signals shared by both, but most explicitly realized in V-01:

1. **Bidirectional LEDGER closure** — V-01 closes the LEDGER in both directions: "findings that don't appear here cannot appear in the narrative" (closed-world from narrative to LEDGER) combined with LEDGER as the exhaustive extraction target (closed-world from artifacts to LEDGER). Neither direction is implicit.

2. **Named output sections as audit surfaces** — V-01's explicit "These are not notes — they are named output sections of the final artifact" converts pre-composition from a cognitive process into a verifiable document section. A reviewer can confirm BLOCK P existed before narrative without inferring from prose style.

3. **Non-paraphrase constraint on BLOCK B** — "do not paraphrase" closes the last degree of freedom in the analytical-to-narrative handoff. Prior rubric versions required an explicit bridge (C-11/C-17); V-01 requires the *same sentence*, eliminating interpretive drift.
