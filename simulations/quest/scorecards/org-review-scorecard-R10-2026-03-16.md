**Pattern 2 — §17 Lens Applicability Protocol (C-33)**
Fourth `Applicability` column in §5.7 LENS COVERAGE TABLE. Rating is `HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW`, artifact-specific (not inherited from role definition). Same role reviewing a different artifact may carry a different rating. Coverage expectation by tier: HIGH-applicability OPEN → ADVISORY-OPEN-LENS; MEDIUM-applicability OPEN → ADVISORY-LOW; LOW-applicability OPEN → no action. Provides the source data tier for §18.

No gate verdict, no ledger row; §6 exclusion list updated. §18 cannot execute without §17 — dependency is declared explicitly.
