## Excellence Signals from V-05

**E-01 — Three ceiling criteria at structurally independent lifecycle positions.**
V-05 achieves C-25/C-26/C-27 simultaneously with zero mutual interference because each operates at a distinct phase: per-path inertia checklist fires pre-Phase 0; quoted extract fires at Pass 3 (pre-Phase 4); exclusion constraint fires at Amendment 1 (Phase 5). The three axes compose cleanly because they are separated by entire execution phases.

**E-02 — Canonical term naming is structurally more constraining than behavioral description.**
V-03 and V-05 (PASS on C-27) use labeled sections — **Signal-skip**, **Nominal gap framing**, **Non-trivial gaming** — before the definitions. V-01/V-02/V-04 describe the same behaviors without using the terms. The canonical name creates a commitment anchor: the model is acknowledging a named failure mode, not just promising diligence. Per-path confirmations compound this — three separate acknowledgments vs. one omnibus confirmation.

**E-03 — Quoted extract as structural post-merge gate removes the pre-fill window.**
Requiring verbatim 3-10 words from merged prose in the integration column (C-26) makes it structurally impossible to complete the Pass 3 table before executing the merge. A Y/N column can be prefilled in the same cognitive pass as Pass 1 and Pass 2; a quoted extract from merged text cannot exist before the merge does. V-01/V-04/V-05 exploit this asymmetry to verify genuine post-merge execution without requiring external audit.

**E-04 — Exclusion constraint with explicit category list makes non-fixed-category diagnosis tractable.**
V-02/V-04/V-05 pair the "must be OUTSIDE" constraint with a *Why this constraint matters* block that enumerates the non-fixed categories: Background epistemic register, Claim scope/specificity, Method framing relative to claim strength, contribution incrementality framing. Without this enumeration, "outside the fixed categories" is a prohibition; with it, it becomes a positive search space. The model knows where to look, not just where not to look.
