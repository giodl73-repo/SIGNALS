### Excellence Signals

Patterns from the top-scoring cluster that improve on the baseline:

1. **Inertia framing (V-05)**: Naming "no change to manuscript" as the default competitor for P1 concerns reframes the task. The author must OVERCOME inertia for P1s, not merely justify their position. This is a conceptual shift, not a formatting addition.

2. **Cover-letter-first commitment anchor (V-01, V-05)**: Writing the cover letter before decomposition commits the author to a revision strategy before individual responses can erode it. The amendment pass then reconciles the commitment against what was actually delivered.

3. **Severity-sorted concern cards with tier headers (V-02, V-05)**: Inserting `--- P1 CONCERNS ---` headers before the response section structurally prevents P1s from being processed with the same weight as editorial notes.
