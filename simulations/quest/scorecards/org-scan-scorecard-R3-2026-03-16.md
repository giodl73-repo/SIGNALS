### Excellence Signals from V-05

**Signal 1 — Anti-fabrication extended to recommendation sections**
V-04 places anti-fabrication notes in A.1, A.2, A.3, B.1 (4 sections). V-05 adds a note to B.2 ("Anti-fabrication: Justification must cite a specific Group A finding. General org design reasoning without scan reference does not meet this step."). The org shape recommendation is as evidence-dependent as the seam candidates — V-05 closes that gap.

**Signal 2 — Gate confirmation sentence carries path floor status**
V-04 confirmation: `"Gate clear — [N]/7 sources found, [N] paths cited, [N] absent."`
V-05 confirmation: `"Gate clear — [N]/7 sources found, [N] paths cited, [N] absent. Path floor: [met / not met — N available]."`
The addition makes the gate's path floor evaluation auditable in the artifact, not just in the agent's internal check.

**Signal 3 — Checklist item names its gate type**
V-05 labels item 4 as `**Path floor gate**:` — the bold label inside the checklist item makes the blocking posture machine-readable at the item level, not just at the gate block level.
