## Excellence Signals (from top-scoring variations)

1. **Inertia gap bookending** (V-05): Declaring why the powerful tool is needed *before* any role executes, then closing that declaration with a verdict in synthesis, creates a structural differentiation proof that instructions alone cannot. Applicable to any skill that competes with a simpler alternative.

2. **Feed-forward ledger table** (V-02): An explicit audit table in the synthesis section that cross-checks plan contracts against execution makes compliance scannable without reading prose. Converts a behavioral expectation into a verifiable artifact.

3. **Per-experiment COMPLETE + Consumed-by routing** (V-03): Tracking feed-forward at the individual experiment level (not just at phase level) catches mid-program deviations before they reach synthesis.

4. **FORBIDDEN clauses per role** (V-05): Negative-space framing (what a role *may not* do) is structurally stronger than positive instructions alone for preventing role bleed in sequenced prompts.
