### Excellence Signals from V-05

**Signal 1: Gravity-accuracy reconciliation as a falsifiable check**

The SCHEDULE RECONCILIATION RESULT gains a "Gravity accuracy check" row: *"did high-gravity roles (4–5) cluster in later months as predicted?"* This converts the gravity model from a pre-simulation input into a testable prediction — the gravity assessment commits to a *distribution* (high-gravity → late), and the reconciliation confirms or refutes that distribution claim. This is structurally analogous to C-16's committed-month reconciliation but operating at the cohort level rather than the individual role level. It is a distinct new pattern: **gravity-model accuracy audit**, where the pre-simulation dependency model is treated as a falsifiable forecast about which roles will form the chasm barrier, not merely as a source for committed month assignments.

**Signal 2: Four-phase C-11 citation chain**

V-05 introduces explicit in-stream checks for C-11 at four independent points: PHASE 1 (incumbent pull cell), PHASE 2 (Gap to EM field), PHASE 3 (Cause element), PHASE 5 (non-adopter reason). C-11's pass condition requires 3+ downstream sections; V-05's PCL architecture monitors the chain phase-by-phase, making threading a *live compliance obligation* rather than a post-hoc count. This is a new structural pattern — **phased citation chain monitoring** — that transforms a single criterion into a multi-point accountability sequence.
