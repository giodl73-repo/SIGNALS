## C-58 Discriminator Detail

The R19 discriminator for C-58 is the universalization of C-57 across all five structural variants. In R18, V-05 was the sole source for C-57, establishing that the explicit compensation foreclosure label was achievable but not confirming it as stable across axis variants. The V-05 form ("a larger or higher-quality inertia-absent evidence base cannot exceed the inertia-absent ceiling — evidence volume does not compensate for inertia absence") demonstrated two structural improvements over the standard C-53 conditional form used in V-01–V-04 R18: it names both compensatory dimensions ("larger or higher-quality") rather than just volume, and it provides an explicit closure label ("evidence volume does not compensate for inertia absence") rather than relying on the reader to infer the foreclosed inference class from the conditional clause.

R19 resolves the stability question: V-01–V-04 adopt the explicit closure label form, upgrading from the standard conditional "even if the evidence base is large." With C-56 PASS for all five (carried from R18 — the structural ceiling chain was already complete) and C-57 now PASS for all five (V-01–V-04 upgraded, V-05 carried), C-58 — their conjunction — is PASS for all five simultaneously for the first time. The axis coverage is now complete: role-sequence (V-01), output-format (V-02), lifecycle-emphasis (V-03), inertia-primary (V-04), and descriptive-register (V-05) all carry the full explicit closure label form alongside the complete ceiling structural form.

The discriminating failure for C-58 is any synthesis that satisfies C-56 without C-57 — the structural ceiling chain is intact but the independence statement uses only the implicit conditional form. This is the R18 V-01–V-04 pattern: Ceiling Declaration first, merged Verdict/Confidence, independence statement present — but the foreclosed inference class named only by implication. The single missing element is the explicit label. C-58 captures the progression: C-53 establishes independence, C-57 labels the class foreclosed by independence, C-56 confirms the structural ceiling chain is complete, and C-58 confirms all three language-and-structure properties are simultaneously satisfied.
