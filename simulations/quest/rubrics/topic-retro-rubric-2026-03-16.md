**Design notes:**

- **5 essential criteria** — inventory (C-01), predicted vs actual (C-02), gaps (C-03), Echo (C-04), accuracy verdict (C-05). All five are load-bearing: a retro missing any one is structurally broken.
- **Echo gets its own essential slot (C-04)** because it's the irreducible learning mechanism of the format — not just a nice-to-have section.
- **AMEND behavior (C-08)** is recommended rather than essential: it only applies when an AMEND is present, so it's never a blocking failure on a standard run.
- **Aspirational criteria push toward calibration** — comparing retros across time (C-09) and converting Echo into signal design changes (C-10) are the bar for a mature practice, not a single output.
