**5 essential** criteria cover the hard requirements: correct committee type instantiation, role-appropriate voice per participant, explicit decisions, action items with owners, and dissent when tension exists.

**3 recommended** raise structural fidelity: minutes format, committee-type-specific discussion depth, and AMEND behavior.

**2 aspirational** push toward genuine value: a pre-mortem risk the real meeting would miss, and traceable charter compliance (quorum, veto, escalation).

The AMEND criteria (C-08) is vacuously satisfied when not invoked, so it doesn't penalize outputs that simply weren't asked to amend — appropriate given the optional nature of that feature.
