**10 criteria across 3 tiers:**

| Tier | Criteria | Weight |
|------|----------|--------|
| Essential (5) | C-01 volume, C-02 idea anatomy, C-03 top-5 marker, C-04 inertia check, C-05 AMEND section | 60% |
| Recommended (3) | C-06 category grouping, C-07 rationale specificity, C-08 category diversity | 30% |
| Aspirational (2) | C-09 AMEND actionability, C-10 top-5 defensibility | 10% |

**Golden threshold**: all 5 essential pass + composite >= 80. The minimum passing combo is essential + all recommended (90 points). Essential-only scores 60 — not golden, so rationale quality and grouping matter.

The trickiest essential is **C-04** (inertia check) — it requires a named do-nothing candidate with a real status-quo rationale, not just a token entry. The trickiest aspirational is **C-10** — it catches outputs where the `**` markers were placed arbitrarily rather than from genuine viability ranking.
