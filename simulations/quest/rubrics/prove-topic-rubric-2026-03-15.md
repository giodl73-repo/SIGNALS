| C-02 | Hypothesis grounded in scout signals | essential | correctness |

**Golden threshold**: all 5 essential pass + composite >= 80 (max 100).

The essential tier focuses on the three things that make this skill an orchestrator rather than a single-step skill: all stages ran (C-01), context was loaded from scout (C-02), and artifacts were written progressively (C-03). C-04/C-05 ensure the terminal output is usable and discoverable. The recommended tier adds traceability and downstream handoff. The aspirational tier targets the two failure modes unique to orchestration: confidence inflation from thin evidence and opaque progress.
