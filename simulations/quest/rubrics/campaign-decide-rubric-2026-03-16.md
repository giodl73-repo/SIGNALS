**Structure summary:**

- **5 essential** (C-01–C-05): recommendation present, confidence stated, all six sub-skill domains covered, inertia-first competitor framing, evidence-to-recommendation traceability
- **3 recommended** (C-06–C-08): structured document format, risk/counter-evidence acknowledged, hypothesis disposition explicit
- **2 aspirational** (C-09–C-10): confidence calibration narrative, actionable next steps conditioned on the recommendation

The hardest essential to satisfy in practice is **C-04** (inertia-first) — it's a specific orchestration constraint that generic decision briefs routinely skip. **C-05** (traceability) is the most important: a floating recommendation with no evidence chain is the core failure mode this skill was designed to prevent.
