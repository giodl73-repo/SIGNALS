**Structure summary:**

- **5 essential** (C-01–C-05): complete skill bodies (not diffs), label+axis+hypothesis on every variation, single-axis isolation, correct N count, genuine distinctness
- **3 recommended** (C-06–C-08): axis spread across ≥3 of the 6 axes, falsifiable hypotheses, explicit/inferable baseline
- **2 aspirational** (C-09–C-10): combination roadmap appended, hypothesis tension surfaced

The two hardest-to-satisfy essential criteria are **C-01** (truly runnable body, not a diff) and **C-03** (exactly one axis changes per variation) — both directly enforce the core promise of the skill. The evaluator notes flag these explicitly for reviewers.
