## What changed in v19

Two new criteria extracted from R20 excellence signals:

**C-49 -- Phase-1 Open Lens Direction Echo** *(r19 aspirational, 2.5 pts)*

Phase 1 opening tag carries each lens name **plus a direction axis** (e.g., `(1) [Lens name] -- [direction]`), not only lens names. V-02/V-03 names-only form is the contrast baseline. Sharpens C-48 (floor: names present; ceiling: names + direction present).

**C-50 -- Phase-0 Open Lens-Name Carry** *(r19 aspirational, 2.5 pts)*

Phase 0 opening tag carries the active lens names from Pre-Phase (e.g., `Active lenses: (1)...(2)...(3)...`), bridging Pre-Phase output into the inertia-baseline phase at its entry point. V-01/V-03 bare Phase 0 open is the contrast baseline. Sharpens C-38 (floor: Phase 0 doubly-marked; ceiling: Phase 0 open carries lens-name roster).

**Independence confirmed:** V-01 passes C-49 only; V-02 passes C-50 only; V-04 and V-05 pass both; V-03 passes neither.

**Lineage:**

- C-50 is a content-sharpening of C-38 Phase-0-open (bare → lens-name carry)
- C-49 and C-50 are coordinate siblings
