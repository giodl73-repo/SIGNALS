## Two new criteria extracted from R18 excellence signals

| ID | Sharpens | Gap closed | Source |
|----|----------|-----------|--------|
| **C-56** | C-49 | Purpose frame vs. forward index — C-49 requires declaring *why* the document exists; C-56 requires enumerating *which* IR-NNs it overrides, so the model enters INERTIA REGISTER with a closed list rather than discovering the enumeration by parsing | ES-1 (reading-register effect: "changes the reading register for all 47 subsequent criteria") |
| **C-57** | C-55 | Selective citation vs. exhaustive declaration — C-55 requires FER-NN cross-citation where detectable; C-57 requires an explicit "No FER entry ([reason])" note where not detectable, making silence structurally impossible | V-05 honest scoping at PCR-03/PCR-04 |

**Denominator**: 47 → 49  
**Formula**: `aspirational_pass / 49 × 10`

The two criteria are structurally independent sharpenings at opposite ends of the registry chain: C-56 refines the opener (before any schema), C-57 refines the terminal layer (PCR FER completeness). A prompt can satisfy C-56 without C-57 (enumerates IR-NNs in opener, but PCR has silent omissions) and vice versa.
