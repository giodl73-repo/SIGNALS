Two new criteria added:

**C-24 (Terminal Document-Close Reconciler)**: V-01's Role 11 is the source. It operates retroactively over the complete document state — distinct from C-22's forward per-gate currency checks. A document with full C-22 coverage but no reconciler has no audit of whether markers were correctly placed; a reconciler catches violations that slipped past incomplete gate coverage. The criterion requires the reconciler to produce a named finding record, not merely scan. Dependency chain: C-18 + C-21.

**C-25 (Two-Tier Violation Taxonomy)**: V-02's STRUCTURAL/CONTENT distinction is the source. C-16 requires "a rejection clause"; C-23 requires a DERIVATION CHAIN rejection clause. Neither requires the Format Contract to name and separate violation types by detection method and remediation path. Structural = scan-time detection, add the column. Content = read-time assessment, deepen the cell. Combining them into one clause conflates two different evaluator operations. Dependency chain: C-16 + C-23.

The aspirational denominator in the scoring formula updates from 15 to 17.
