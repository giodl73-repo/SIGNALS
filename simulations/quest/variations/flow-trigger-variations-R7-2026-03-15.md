## R7 Variation Summary

All five variations carry the full R6 foundation (C-01 through C-16) plus treat C-17/C-18/C-19 as required baseline. The variation axes:

| Variation | Axis | Core Hypothesis |
|-----------|------|----------------|
| **V-01** | Role sequence — Preamble Architect authority contract | Concentrating all three pre-enumeration artifacts (EOR table, depth budget, exclusion log) in one dedicated role makes omission equivalent to a named role violation. Absence of any artifact blocks role completion gate. |
| **V-02** | Output format — Protocol Ledger (one artifact, three indexed sections) | Merging EOR table + exclusion log + depth budget into a single named PROTOCOL LEDGER with a composite `Ledger ref:` citation slot makes all three preamble criteria fail or pass together. One artifact is harder to partially omit than three separate ones. |
| **V-03** | Lifecycle emphasis — compact preamble, maximal entry template | Compress preamble to minimum required declarations; invest structurally in the entry template (all citation slots inline, all slots named). Tests whether a dense template compensates for a lean preamble. |
| **V-04** | Phrasing register — instructional advisory throughout | All structural obligations phrased as build-step guidance ("Build your EOR table here...") rather than prescriptive mandates (SHALL/MUST). Tests whether structural forms enforce compliance regardless of register. |
| **V-05** | Inertia framing + combined R6 foundations | Names three specific default failure modes (recall ordering / unbounded cascades / silent scope) simultaneously; overrides each with the structural mechanism that closes it; labels the override as active/inactive per entry. Makes the connection between the rule and the failure it prevents explicit. |

**Key structural novelties in R7:**
- V-01's Preamble Architect creates a **single authority contract** for all three preamble artifacts — miss one and the role gate fails
- V-02's Protocol Ledger introduces a **composite citation** (`Ledger ref: EOR-{NN}`) that satisfies C-17 and C-19 in one slot
- V-05 labels overrides **inline per entry** (`[Depth: N/MAX — OVERRIDE-2 active]`) so compliance is self-certifying at the row level
