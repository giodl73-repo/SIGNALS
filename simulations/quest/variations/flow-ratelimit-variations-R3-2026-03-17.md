## R3 Variate Summary

Five variations, each targeting all four R2 gaps (C-10, C-12, C-13/C-15, C-16). Single-axis for V-01–V-03, combination for V-04–V-05.

| | V-01 | V-02 | V-03 | V-04 | V-05 |
|---|------|------|------|------|------|
| **Axis** | Role sequence | Output format | Lifecycle emphasis | Phrasing register | Inertia framing |
| **C-15 mechanism** | Role 1 gates all others — Role 3+ blocked until Verdict exists | "PREAMBLE A" label + "before any numbered section" | Phase 0 gets most instruction weight + explicit gate-close | "Before you write a single analysis sentence" imperative | "Write first, before any inventory or table" tied to INERTIA framing |
| **C-16 mechanism** | Role 2 (Format Contract Writer) as gated preamble role | "PREAMBLE B" with scenario-specific meanings + rejection clause | Phase 0.5 with "At least two distinct Phase tables must comply" | "Before your first table, declare what your tables mean" | FORMAT CONTRACT names INERTIA explicitly as competitor state |
| **C-10 mechanism** | Role 5 arithmetic mandate with "qualitative-only rows fail" | Section 5 arithmetic + "qualitative-only cells are INCOMPLETE under FORMAT CONTRACT" | Phase 5 arithmetic + INCOMPLETE flag | "Do the math — show the calculation — qualitative-only rows fail" | 5x INERTIA column obligation ("INCOMPLETE under FORMAT CONTRACT") |
| **C-12 mechanism** | FORMAT CONTRACT on Role 5 volume table | BASELINE/PROTECTED schema in Section 5 | Phase 0.5 schema applied to Phase 5 | FORMAT CONTRACT applied to Step 5 | Structural — every table is INERTIA/PROTECTED by design |

**Distinguishing bets:**
- **V-01**: Role gating is the most mechanically hard — the model literally cannot output Role 3 until Role 1 exists
- **V-03**: Phase 0 instruction density (most words in prompt) signals primacy to the model
- **V-04**: Imperative time-index ("before a single sentence", "now") acts earlier in generation than structural labels
- **V-05**: INERTIA framing makes C-12/C-14 fall out for free — no special instructions needed, the frame requires it
