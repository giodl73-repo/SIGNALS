### Key structural differences per variation

**V-01** — Identical structure to R3 V-05; only the connecting prose changes register. Every fill-contract block is retained verbatim. The PREAMBLE, SECTION 1, phase month blocks, and SCHEDULE RECONCILIATION are all framed as analyst judgments ("your committed schedule", "before the simulation runs, you declare…").

**V-02** — Adds a `DEPENDENCY GRAVITY ASSESSMENT` block to the PREAMBLE (rate each role 1–5 for daily reliance on THE INCUMBENT). SECTION 1's "Incumbent dependency" column becomes "gravity N — note". SCHEDULE RECONCILIATION gains a Gravity column; deviation reasons must cite gravity score + THE INCUMBENT. The chasm table adds a "Gravity cluster" row (which high-gravity roles constitute the EM barrier). SECTION 5 churn register gains a gravity column.

**V-03** — Adds a `PHASE N COMPLIANCE LEDGER` block after each PHASE CLOSE. Each PCL checks 5–6 specific criterion slots (C-16a/b/c/d/e/f broken into sub-checks) with PASS/FAIL/PARTIAL. Any FAIL must be corrected before the next phase header. This makes structural compliance an in-stream obligation rather than a post-hoc audit.

**V-05** — Full combination. PCL checks in V-03 gain a V-02 check (gravity score present, gravity accuracy check in SCHEDULE RECONCILIATION RESULT). PHASE 3 table gains the gravity cluster row. SECTION 5 churn register has gravity column. Narrative framing throughout.
