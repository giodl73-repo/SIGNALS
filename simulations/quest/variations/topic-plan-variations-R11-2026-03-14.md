## Round 11 Variations — topic:plan (v10 rubric, 235 pts)

---

### Design rationale

R10 V-03 achieved the first perfect 27/27 aspirational pass. The two new v10 criteria (C-36, C-37) *promote* what V-03 introduced — the COLUMN CONTRACT architecture and the decision-question independence test. R11 builds on that baseline rather than re-proving it.

**Three single-axis dimensions:**

| # | Axis | What it changes | Target |
|---|------|-----------------|--------|
| V-01 | **Role sequence** | Adds Step 0 "Hypothesis Generator" before any file read. Delta Findings gain a "Hypothesis match" column: confirmed vs. surprise. | Delta quality: a surprise finding (no matching hypothesis) is the highest-value discovery |
| V-02 | **Inertia framing** | Opens with "current strategy is the status quo / each proposal is a challenger" framing. Renames "If unchanged" as "Status quo retains." Adds a pre-proposal Inertia Cost Audit table (Step 7a) before proposals. | C-25/C-31/C-32 load-bearing quality — proposals as arguments, not signal translations |
| V-03 | **Output format** | Symmetric PASS/FAIL exhibits for **all** operationalized tests: Rule 1 columns (Type, Reversibility, Delta candidate?, Consistent with strategy?), Rule 2 (Implicit evidence), and the anti-pattern checkpoint at the delta step. | C-37 completeness — bidirectional exhibits block novel shortcuts that recognition-only scaffolds miss |

**Two combination variations:**

| # | Axes combined | Key mechanism |
|---|--------------|---------------|
| V-04 | Role sequence + Output format | V-01 hypothesis step + V-03 symmetric exhibits |
| V-05 | All three + expanded 4-rule COLUMN CONTRACT | Rule 3 (evidence provenance) + Rule 4 (null completeness) elevated from per-step to pre-schema contract; all three single-axis mechanisms |

---

### COLUMN CONTRACT architecture across all five variations

All five maintain:
- Named COLUMN CONTRACT block **above** the output schema (C-36 — architectural positioning)
- Decision-question independence test in `Implicit evidence` column definition (C-37 — prospective, cell-level)
- Closed value lists at both the upfront schema and commitment checkpoints for all enumerated columns (C-34)

V-05 expands to 4 rules: Rule 3 (evidence provenance — cells must trace to artifact filenames, not other output columns) and Rule 4 (null-case completeness — every mandatory section has a verbatim null template with a "Do not omit" instruction).
