## R11 — corps-scan — 5 Variations

All five target v10 (250 pts, C-01 through C-40). Since C-38/C-39/C-40 are now rubric invariants, every variation satisfies all three — the axes govern **how**, not whether.

| # | Axis | Key Mechanism |
|---|------|---------------|
| V-01 | **Hypothesis formalism** | C-38 as IF/PREDICT/FALSIFIED-BY triplet; C-39 entries typed as `IS-FALSIFICATION-SIGNAL — CONFIRMED|OVERTURNED`; C-40 names the signal outcome in AMEND-A |
| V-02 | **Weighted evidence table** | Deliberation replaces prose tri-part with a scoring table (Base/Penalty/Net); DARK SIGNALS labels forced into the Penalty column, making C-37 non-bypassable; C-40 reports Net Score delta |
| V-03 | **Amend-as-verdict ordering** | C-40 hypothesis verdict is the **first field** of every amend option; C-16 debt framing is secondary; closing Chain Audit block traces C-38→C-39→C-40 in one place |
| V-04 | **Numbered scan walk protocol** | SCAN is 5 explicit steps scoped to artifact classes (dirs, manifests, workspace config, domain language, exec signal); each step yields a typed Signal Schema row or DARK SIGNALS entry; Step 5 emits hypothesis outcome |
| V-05 | **Combination: V-01 + V-02 + V-03** | Structured claim (C-38) → IS-FALSIFICATION-SIGNAL field (C-39) → Penalty column forces labels (C-37) → verdict-first amend + chain audit (C-40) |
