# flow-dataflow Round 11 Variations — Summary

**New criterion targeted**: C-24 — Pre-declared complete output scaffold (table number, name, purpose, upstream table references declared before first stage trace).

**Key distinction from R9 ARTIFACT REGISTRY**: C-24 requires the **upstream table references column** — a dependency graph between tables, not just a section inventory. This is what makes the scaffold a resolvable navigational contract for C-15/C-16/C-18/C-19/C-20/C-23 cross-table citations.
