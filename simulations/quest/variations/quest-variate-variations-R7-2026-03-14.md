### V-01 — Baseline: R7 full stack (C-24 + C-25 + C-26 integrated)

SetCoherenceAuditor persona with pre-Phase-1 obligation installation (C-25). Planning table with precision column header: *"name one specific structural property whose absence would clearly falsify this claim"* (C-24). Phase 1 STOP CONDITION with explicit V-ID citation gate (C-26). Set-level checkpoint predicates (C-21). Imperative failure branch STOP AND REWRITE (C-23). All stop conditions imperative (C-10).

**Hypothesis:** Primary-effect fields will name specific structural artifacts (checkpoint row types, gate keywords, column names) — C-24 emerges from compound activation of C-25 role prior + precision column header. Secondary: preamble elaboration crowds primary-effect sharpness in later slots, but column header provides a structural floor. Predicted sites: V-04 shows V-ID citation gaps; V-05 shows directional claims.

### V-02 — C-27 ablation candidate: Phase 2 STOP CONDITION advisory

**Single change from V-01:** Phase 2 STOP CONDITION becomes *"Please verify all 5 variations have passed before proceeding"* (advisory). Phase 1 gate and per-variation checkpoint remain imperative and unchanged.

**Hypothesis:** Phase 3 entry will occur in some runs with marginal checkpoint passes — the advisory Phase 2 gate creates a "proceed and note" window absent from V-01. Phase 1 and checkpoint compliance remain equivalent, so degradation isolates to the Phase 2→3 boundary specifically. Predicted sites: V-01 provides the direct contrast; V-01/V-02 comparison isolates Phase 2 register as the C-27 candidate mechanism.

### V-03 — C-25 ablation: VariationScientist without preamble obligations (C-22 path 2 to C-21)

**Single change from V-01:** VariationScientist persona; preamble describes capabilities, not role-constitutive obligations. Phase 1 V-ID gate (C-26) retained. Set-level checkpoint predicates (C-21) retained — labeled "Set-level checks" rather than "your professional obligation as SetCoherenceAuditor." C-24 precision column header retained. C-22: V-01 achieves C-21 via role prior (C-25) + format; V-03 achieves C-21 via format alone — structurally independent paths.

**Hypothesis:** C-26 compliance maintained at equivalent rates to V-01 (gate is structural, not role-dependent). Without C-25, planning-table entries are shorter with less cross-variation elaboration — primary-effect sharpness improves while secondary-effect richness decreases. Predicted sites: V-01 shows longer elaboration; V-04 (C-25 without C-26) shows lower V-ID compliance than V-03.

### V-04 — C-26 ablation: Phase 1 V-ID gate removed, preamble obligations retained

**Single change from V-01:** Phase 1 STOP CONDITION gates on template completion + ≥4 distinct axes only — V-ID citation in "Predicted manifestation site" NOT checked. C-25 preamble obligations retained. C-21 post-generation checkpoint predicate for V-ID citation retained.

**Hypothesis:** V-ID citation gaps in planning tables occur more frequently than V-01 — preamble obligation (C-25) alone is insufficient to enforce V-ID citation as reliably as the Phase 1 structural gate. C-21 catches gaps post-generation (retroactive enforcement vs. V-01's preventive gate), increasing iteration cost. Predicted sites: V-01 (C-25+C-26), V-03 (C-26 only), V-04 (C-25 only) form a 3-cell dissociation of independent contributions.

---

### V-05 — C-24 ablation: primary-effect column header directional

**Single change from V-01:** Primary-effect column header becomes *"describe the expected behavioral change this axis produces"*. Phase 1 STOP CONDITION drops the precision falsifiability requirement (condition 2), retains template completion + V-ID citation checks. C-25 preamble, C-26 V-ID gate, C-21 checkpoint, C-23 imperative failure — all identical to V-01.

**Hypothesis:** Planning-table primary-effect fields contain directional claims ("will produce more coherent variations") rather than specific structural property predictions — C-20 satisfied (column present and non-empty), C-24 fails. SetCoherenceAuditor role prior partially compensates, creating higher run-to-run variance than V-01. Predicted sites: V-01 (same role prior, precision header) shows specificity V-05 lacks; comparison isolates column-header instruction as C-24's proximal mechanism.
