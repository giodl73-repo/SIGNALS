## R3 Variation Summary

| Variation | Axis | Target | Hypothesis |
|-----------|------|--------|------------|
| **V-01** | Calibration block (explicit printed artifact) | C-14 | R2 V-01's silent triage gave no verifiable evidence. A printed Calibration Block commits the distribution before any finding — making C-14 structurally verifiable, not inferred from outcome |
| **V-02** | Update Protocol as structured process table | C-15 | R2 V-03's prose Update Protocol could be satisfied by paraphrasing. A table with three mandatory columns (Trigger Events / Owner Role / Revisit Cadence) makes C-15 pass by cell existence |
