## Round 5 — listen-adoption Variations

**Rubric:** v5 | **New criteria targeted:** C-17 (Preamble Citation Contract) + C-18 (Dependency Gravity Assessment)

---

### Variation Axes

| # | Axis | Target | Device |

### Key Structural Differences from R4

**C-17 gap closed by V-01/V-04/V-05:** R4 V-01 had a pipe-list citation contract that was forward-declared but not auditable as a structured check. The CITATION CONTRACT TABLE makes each obligation a named row with a Met? column that the model fills in CONTRACT COMPLIANCE AUDIT — converting a prose declaration into a verifiable grid.

**C-18 gap closed by V-02/V-04/V-05:** R4 V-02 had `gravity [G]: [reason]` format but didn't enforce specificity of the reason. The typed citation rule `G[N]: [exact behavior THE INCUMBENT had for this role]` forces the deviation reason to trace back to the specific basis column entry from DEPENDENCY GRAVITY ASSESSMENT.

**C-18 chasm row upgraded by V-03/V-05:** R4 had a "Gravity cluster" row in the chasm table. V-03 elevates this to a first-class GRAVITY CLUSTER IDENTIFICATION block with cluster inertia mechanism + PHASE 4 ENTRY GATE, making the cluster's unlocking a live simulation event rather than a static annotation.
