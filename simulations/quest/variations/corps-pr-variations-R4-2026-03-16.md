## What R4 closes that R3 left open

**C-15 (domain-lens rewrite gate):** R3 variations had a validity check but expressed it as a column or prose note. R4 V-01/V-03/V-04 express it as a named two-step procedure: Step A (binary test) → Step B (rewrite consequence with explicit reason why specificity alone doesn't satisfy it). V-02/V-05 put both steps inline in the Domain-Lens column as distinct cells.

**C-16 (AMEND as structured template):** R3 V-03 had AMEND prose instructions ("state what role was added..."). All five R4 variations use labeled `Field: Value` slot notation for the AMEND block — a schema that makes missing fields structurally visible.

**C-13 (ban list depth):** R3 variations enumerated >=3 banned phrases as checklists. R4 V-03/V-05 add the required replacement form beside each banned phrase, making the ban list a substitution protocol rather than a prohibition-only checklist.
