### Variation summary

| V | Axis | Hypothesis |
|---|------|------------|
| **V-01** | Role sequence — stakeholder table BEFORE decision table in Phase 1 | Stakeholder-first forces people-centric derivation of Decision-Maker Roles, closing F-05 Consumer coverage gaps missed by feature-centric decision generation |

| **V-04** | Axes 1+2 — stakeholder-first + second-person | Tests whether role sequence benefits survive phrasing register change; isolates the interaction term |
| **V-05** | All three — stakeholder-first + second-person + INERTIA REGISTER | Maximum-surface combination; unique signal is IR-citation language in second-person register ("you will have reproduced IR-03") |
