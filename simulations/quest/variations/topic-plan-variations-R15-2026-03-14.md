**Round 15 — 5 variations against v14 rubric (280 pts, C-01–C-46)**

All R15 variations preserve the R14 V-01 floor: 4-rule CONTRACT, C-44 "non-withdrawn" at Step 9, C-45 Rule 4 in CONTRACT, C-42/C-43 symmetric PASS/FAIL pairs.

| Variation | Axis | Key structural change |
|-----------|------|-----------------------|

**Key differences from R14:**
- R14 explored lifecycle gates (V-01), unified exhibit (V-02), and pre-emptive threshold Defender (V-03). R15 does not repeat those axes in isolation.
- The ante-signal Defense Register (V-01 R15) differs from R14 V-03's pre-emptive warrant: R14 V-03 set evidence thresholds *before* proposals; R15 V-01 pre-registers *defenses* before signals. The warrant gate filters proposals at build-time; the Defense Register strengthens the post-build challenge.
- V-05 adds a Phase 4 gate (new in R15) covering the build phase — R14 V-01 had no gate after Phase 3.
