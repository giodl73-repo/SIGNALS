## scout-inertia -- Quest Variate R14 Summary

**Rubric**: C-series v14 | **New criteria**: C-45, C-46, C-47, C-48 | **Denominator**: 36 → 40

### Variation Summary

| V | Axis | C-45 Gate Form | C-46 Compound Term | C-47/C-48 |
|---|------|---------------|-------------------|-----------|
| V-01 | Inertia framing | `BOTH Q3 AND Q4 STATUS QUO COMPETITOR BRIDGES` | STATUS QUO COMPETITOR | Section 0 + Section 4 directive |
| V-02 | Lifecycle emphasis (reference) | `BOTH Q3 AND Q4 INERTIA DEFAULT COMPETITOR BRIDGES` | INERTIA DEFAULT COMPETITOR | Stage 0 + Stage 5A directive |

| V-05 | All axes combined | `BOTH Q3 AND Q4 DO-NOTHING DEFAULT BRIDGES` (C-46 axis vocab reused as artifact class) | DO-NOTHING DEFAULT COMPETITOR | Section 0 + Section 4 directive |

### Key design decisions

**C-45**: Two structural forms tested — artifact IDs before the class noun (`Q3 AND Q4 [CLASS]`, V-01/V-02/V-05) vs parenthetical class labels per artifact (`Q3 (FM-ACTOR-BRIDGE) AND Q4 (FM-TRIGGER-BRIDGE)`, V-03/V-04). Both satisfy C-43 (BOTH = count 2) and C-45 (Q3/Q4 named by ID).

**C-46**: Five distinct compound terms, all sharing the axis+role structure. V-05 is the notable case: "DO-NOTHING DEFAULT" (axis) reused as the artifact-class noun in the gate heading, creating cross-criterion vocabulary coherence.

**C-47 + C-48**: All five variations include a Stage 0/Section 0 pre-assessment table pre-declaring HIGH with a PROVISIONAL mechanism for non-HIGH cases, and an explicit `REVISIT STAGE 0 / RETURN TO SECTION 0` directive at the evidence-assessment stage. V-02 is the reference form (Stage 0 → Stage 5A).
