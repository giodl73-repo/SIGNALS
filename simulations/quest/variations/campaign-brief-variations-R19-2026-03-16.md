## R19 Variations — campaign-brief

**Single-axis (V-01, V-02, V-03):** Vary the depth specification in both companion execution-note absent-state rules, holding everything else constant.

**Combined (V-04, V-05):** Achieve C-38 PASS, then extend beyond minimum-sufficient.

### What R19 resolves

**Primary investigation (C-38 recoverable vs. structural reinforcement):** V-01 under double-elision is the behavioral control. If it produces depth-asymmetric companion output, V-04's parity constraint is a recoverable criterion — it closes an observable gap. If V-01 already achieves full-parity output, C-38 is structural reinforcement.

**Band 2 confirmation:** V-02 and V-03 confirm whether aspirational Band 2 sub-forms (uncalibrated positive-form vs. negative-form depth guidance) score identically to Band 1 or receive a different -10 classification. Both are expected 370/380 — the Band 2 distinction is diagnostic, not penalty-differential.

**C-39 candidate (V-05):** The depth-symmetric non-degradation clause forecloses a failure mode V-04 leaves open — depth-trading (full companion output at the cost of abbreviated present-clause output). The per-companion operationalization converts the comparative parity reference into a direct output specification. If V-04 already forecloses depth-trading, V-05 is structural reinforcement; if depth-trading is observed in V-04 and closed by V-05, the non-degradation clause is C-39 candidate material.
