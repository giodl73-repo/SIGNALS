### Design rationale

**V-01** is the diagnostic control. It carries all four R17 sentinels (C-42/43/44/45 FULL) but leaves the CONVICTION GAP DIAGNOSIS in its R17 form — 6-column, pre-declared rows, register-split columns (C-35 FULL), no in-table enforcement sentinel. This isolates the gap-diagnosis axis and confirms that C-35 satisfaction does not imply C-46.

**V-02** adds the minimum-form CONVICTION GAP DIAGNOSIS RULE sentinel. This is the parallel of R17 V-02 (minimum four-sentinel form) — tests whether terse in-table text is structurally sufficient for C-46.

**V-03** uses conversational register throughout, with a conversational CONVICTION GAP DIAGNOSIS RULE sentinel. Extends D17 register invariance to the gap-diagnosis axis.

**V-04** runs all five sentinels at maximum enforcement density. Tests density invariance for C-46 — if score equals V-02, density is non-load-bearing at the 273 level.

**V-05** compresses all surrounding prose to minimum while keeping all five sentinels. Tests whether adjacent prose volume is load-bearing for C-46/C-47 — the final invariance dimension in the stack.
