### Key structural novelties vs. R19

**V-01 role sequence reversal**: Temporal reliability (when?) is now structurally primary over epistemic basis (how?). Step 3c-staleness carries a forward-reference directive: EXPIRED sources should be tentatively reclassified as ASSUMED in Step 3b. This encodes the dependency in the prompt's ordering, not just its content.

**V-02 checksum matrix**: Step 9b cells carry only audit results (`PASS`/`FAIL`/`N/A:[code]`), not field values. Column-pattern detection is now possible — a field FAIL across all rows signals a structural synthesis gap independent of per-stakeholder quality. Opens path to future per-column zero-FAIL constraints.

**V-03 Step 0c planning layer**: Response Track is no longer a column annotation — it's a planning table with Engagement Objective, Success Criterion, and Downstream Message Requirement per competitor. `FAIL_RESPONSE_TRACK_ALIGNMENT` is now a content-alignment gate (does the message include the required elements?) rather than a labeling gate (is the track cited?). CO-EXIST's "avoid replacement language" is an explicit obligation, not a caution.
