## Round 7 — Five Variations (v6 rubric, C-01–C-27)

### Design Logic

R6 made all four v6 criteria structurally present. R7's question: **are they load-bearing or decorative?** The mechanisms exist — can a model still fill them mechanically without genuine causal reasoning?

Three new dimensions close the remaining gaps:

| Dimension | Gap it closes |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Role sequence** | C-24: "assumption archaeologist" posture before reading produces qualitatively different extractions than "fill the last row" |
| **Reversibility** | C-25: adds a three-value taxonomy (Reversible / Gets harder / Becomes impossible) that forces a trajectory claim, not a current-state synonym |
| **A-to-Proposal traceability chain** | C-16/C-24: A-NN → F-NN → Proposal ID made a named navigation obligation, not just a structurally available path |

### Structural Bets

**V-01** — Role declarations direct attention before execution. The "Assumption Archaeologist" mines differently than a table-filler. C-26/C-27 partial is the deliberate cost — isolates the role-sequence axis.

**V-02** — Conversational register embeds purpose in the framing. "Because every proposal needs to answer: what fails to improve if we defer this?" is harder to discharge mechanically than "Do not omit any column." Sole variation using narrative phrasing for C-21.

**V-03** — Reversibility taxonomy forces a forward causal claim. *Becomes impossible* cannot be pattern-matched from a delta finding — the model must reason about time pressure. If this produces distinctly richer "If unchanged" content, it becomes a C-28 candidate.

**V-04** — Role sequence + schema-first address two independent failure modes: shallow reasoning (role) and column omission under context pressure (schema priming). Neither closes the other's gap.

**V-05** — R7 ceiling. Full stack with reversibility, Archaeologist role carrying both a "Likelihood" prioritization column and a "Contradicted?" back-fill obligation, and Step 6 checkpoint committing to both schema AND null-rows simultaneously. Most likely source of Round 8 excellence signals.
