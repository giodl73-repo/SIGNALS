## corps-scan Variate R5 — Summary

**Target**: 170/170 (all 24 criteria). All four R5 invariants are structural in every variation.

### Variation Axes

| Var | Axis | Key Mechanism |
|-----|------|---------------|
| **V-01** | Role sequence | 4-role cast with a dedicated **Pivot Analyst** role whose sole output is the C-23 deliberation table. C-21/C-22 owned by Archaeologist; C-24 owned by Drafter. |

### How R5 Builds on R4

R4's three-way tie (V-01/V-02/V-03 all at 150/150) was broken by v5 precisely because each variation held one new criterion exclusively. R5 eliminates that gap:

- C-23 (pivot deliberation): R4 V-01 alone had it — now every R5 variation includes a structured deliberation table with row citations
- C-21 (C-NN column labels): R4 V-02 alone had it — now every schema uses labeled columns
- C-22 (section self-labeling): R4 V-02 alone had it — now every variation's schema section self-announces
- C-24 (group-level Inertia Advocate): R4 V-03 alone had it — now every variation's YAML has group-level governance
