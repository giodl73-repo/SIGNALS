**R13 summary**

All 5 variations are designed to achieve 23/23 against v11 and introduce new structural seeds that are orthogonal to all current criteria.

**Seeds formalized in all 5 variations** (v12-C-32/C-33 candidates):
- **Seed A**: `resolves: {flag-name}` on inertia-flag-resolving actions — links the action layer to the trajectory dimension explicitly
- **Seed B**: Pre-synthesis cross-dimensional reconciliation pairing (one Health × one Inertia observation + interaction statement before Team Insight)

**New seeds introduced:**

| Seed | Pattern | Variations | Notes |
|------|---------|-----------|-------|
| H | Severity-ordered health topic reporting (critical → warning → healthy) | V-01, V-04, V-05 | Orthogonal to C-27 (separation) — tests whether *order* of health reporting is severity-driven |
| I | Cross-role inertia/health discrepancy validation table (Assessor flags vs Inspector findings) | V-02, V-05 | New dimension: flag retraction when trajectory claim is contradicted by health data |
| J | Stub-conformance markers `[CONFORMS: {section-name}]` after each stub-driven block | V-03 | Orthogonal to `[ ]` gates — per-section format assertion separate from gate-level content check |
| K | Tiered action output (`[CRITICAL]` / `[WARNING]` / `[ADVANCING]` sections) | V-04, V-05 | Orthogonal to `prevents:` (zero-score) and `resolves:` (flag resolution) — organizes by urgency |
| L | Insight projection test (`projection: local` vs `projection: forward`) | V-05 | New: forces explicit scope bounding — insight must declare whether it applies to existing or anticipated topics |
