### Design Notes

**Single-axis picks:**
- V-01 vs V-02 vs V-04 test three independent forcing mechanisms: semantic framing, structural format, and explicit phase scaffolding.

**Key rubric tensions these target:**
- C-01 vs C-05 confusion — V-03 forces their distinction by having the Advocate describe the workaround positively *before* the Analyst finds its failure modes.
- C-02 quantification failure — V-02 and V-05 use table cells that cannot accept adjectives.
- C-04 vs C-05 conflation — V-04 Phase 3 (break the workaround) and Phase 4 (why inertia loses) are named separately and explicitly distinguished.
- C-10 steelman weakness — V-03 generates a genuine advocate argument before asking for a rebuttal, producing stronger raw material than asking for both in one pass.

**Expected scoring prediction:**
V-05 should score highest on C-01–C-05 (structural enforcement). V-03 should score highest on C-10 (steelman quality). V-02 should score highest on C-06 (dimensions separate). V-04 is the safest baseline — likely to pass all essentials without maximizing aspirationals.
