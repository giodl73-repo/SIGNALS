## R10 — corps-leaderboard Variations

**Design target**: break the v10 ceiling (19/21, sole holder V-02) by closing the C-29 gap in V-02 and building a full-integration V-05.

### Variation axes

| V | Axis | R9 → R10 change | v10 score |
|---|------|-----------------|-----------|
| V-01 | MUST/SHALL phrasing | Add C-29: `[ ]` gate after Section 2 + terminal Section 4 gate | 18/21 (~98.57) |
| V-02 | Archivist → Analyst → Publisher (C-26+C-28) | Add C-29: multi-item `[ ]` gate at each role boundary | **20/21 (~99.52)** |
| V-03 | Gate-driven checklist (C-29) | Add C-27: Phase 2 split into Health Phase (2a) + Inertia Phase (2b) | 19/21 (~99.05) |
| V-04 | Milestones-first lifecycle | Add C-26+C-28: restructure as 2-role Surveyor→Strategist with explicit artifact inventory at handoff | 19/21 (~99.05) |
| V-05 | **Full integration** | C-26 (3-role named pipeline) + C-27 (Health/Inertia in Analyst) + C-28 (artifact inventories) + C-29 (gates at all 3 role boundaries) | **21/21 (~100.00)** |

### What V-05 does to break the ceiling

The Analyst role in V-05 runs two explicitly named, structurally separated sub-phases — the **Health Phase** (current state: counts, coverage, achievements) and the **Inertia Phase** (trajectory: flags like `stuck-at-first`, `solo-hold`, `namespace-thin`). Neither phase may include content belonging to the other. The Analyst Gate checklist verifies separation before the artifact handoff. The handoff enumerates all four transferred artifacts by name (achievement tables, milestone table, inertia table, nearest-miss table). The Publisher's Skeptic gate requires the insight to advance beyond what the full Analyst output reveals. This combines all eight new aspirational criteria (C-22 through C-29) in a single prompt.
