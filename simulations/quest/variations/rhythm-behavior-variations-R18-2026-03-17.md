### Design rationale

R17 closed three significant cells: N=3 all-same-type (C-53), N=5 maximum same-type
density (C-55), N=4 modular reduction (C-52), and position-1 generality across named
section bodies (C-54). What remains open is:

**Synthesis body as placement type.** The five named sub-skill section bodies (C-35) and
the inter-section peer slot (C-24) have been confirmed as qualifying positions. The
Synthesis body has appeared as a structural fixture in every variation but has never
been used as a placement location. C-41 mentions N=6 "including Synthesis body,"
explicitly anticipating this test. V-02 places the rule ONLY in the Synthesis body to
isolate this confirmation.

**N=6 including Synthesis body.** V-03 adds the Synthesis body to the N=5 configuration
from R17 V-05. The sub-skill placements are entirely absorbed by C-55+C-37 (confirmed).
The Synthesis body (position 6) is a new position type; its inertness as a 6th placement
extends the modular-reduction principle to three-position-type compositions (sub-skill
bodies + Synthesis body; the inter-section peer slot is deliberately excluded here to
isolate the Synthesis-body variable). C-57 closes the N=6-with-Synthesis cell.

**Descriptive-first at N>=3.** Every multi-placement N>=3 test across R11-R17 has used
imperative at position 1. C-45 (desc-first N=2 dual section-body) and C-39 (no-N-ceiling
for desc-first) together predict that N=3 descriptive-first composes without interaction.
V-04 is the first explicit test. The registers are desc/imp/desc; position 3 would be
the first desc-subsequent-to-desc in a section-body series, directly engaging C-39.
C-58 closes the N=3 desc-first cell.

**Trace-permissions as position 1.** R17 V-04 tested flow-conversation (2nd in sequence)
as position 1. The remaining extreme case is trace-permissions (5th and last in sequence),
where the maximum number of preceding sub-skill section bodies (four) appear without
the rule. The structural hypothesis is that "first qualifying occurrence" is determined
solely by where the rule first appears, regardless of how many rule-absent section bodies
precede it. V-05 + C-59 close this structural gap for the late-sequence extreme.

**Cross-references to update in v19 rubric:** C-41 (R18 direct test note; add C-57),
C-39 (R18 direct test note; add C-58), C-35 (trace-permissions R18 V-05 note), C-54
(R18 note; extend to trace-permissions extreme-late confirmation via C-59), C-23 (R18
note; Synthesis body satisfies isolation requirement; add C-56), C-37 (R18 note; add
C-57 as N=6 Synthesis-body confirmation).
