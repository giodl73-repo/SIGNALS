### Key structural innovations per variation

**V-01 — IB-as-anchor-zero ordering**: Phase 1 splits into STEP 0a (T-05 produced before scaffold) and STEP 0b (scaffold referencing "Already produced above"). The scaffold's T-05 Declaration Status cell is the enforcement mechanism — a model that writes scaffold first has no valid entry to put there.

**V-02 — IB-Col? / HO-Col? flag columns**: Two new boolean columns on every scaffold row. Every PG-NN row has `IB-Col? = YES` and must declare `IB-NN Coverage` in Mandatory Columns. T-04 has `HO-Col? = YES` and must declare both handoff contract columns. A scaffold row that contradicts its own flag is a CMD-02 breach.

**V-03 — Finance/Operations role split with T-12**: T-12 is a standalone Handoff Continuity Contract table pre-declared in the scaffold. Finance Controller's delivery guarantee and Operations Analyst's acceptance requirement are named artifacts/preconditions, not readiness states. CMD register carries a Role Owner column.

**V-04 — Coaching-imperative with formulas**: The triple-count arithmetic is stated as `M_k = M_(k-1) + N_k`. The IB-NN list rule is stated as a contradiction test. The Responsible Role is framed as "who gets paged at 3am?" The handoff contract is framed as two specific questions.

**V-05 — IB-first + T-03 displacement claims + compound enforcement**: T-03 gains an `IB-NN Steps Replaced` column, forward-declaring displacement claims before any stage trace. Triple-count checkpoints verify those claims. PG-04's IB-NN Coverage must match T-03's declared steps. Every T-10 row's Responsible Role traces back to T-05's Responsible Role column for the cited IB-NN step.
