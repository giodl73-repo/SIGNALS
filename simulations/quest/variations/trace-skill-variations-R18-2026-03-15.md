## trace-skill Variations R18

**Entry state**: R17 V-05 passes C-01 through C-50. Three excellence patterns from R17 are now rubric criteria in v17 (C-51, C-52, C-53). R18 targets them with precision upgrades over R17's partial implementations.

### Variation summary

| Variation | Axis | Core upgrade over R17 |
|-----------|------|-----------------------|
| **V-01** | C-51 single | STRUCTURAL MANDATE (C-49+) becomes fully canonical — independence statement separated from verification instruction as a structurally distinct sentence (TCR component 4 present independently, not merged into component 3) |
| **V-02** | C-52 single | One-step-one-operator discipline — R17's step (b) carried two `-> confirms` on two sub-lines; V-02 splits it into steps (b) and (c) so every step line yields exactly two tokens on `->` field-split |
| **V-03** | C-53 single | TCR Component 6 `(C-14): PHASE LABEL SCHEMA` + SCORER HEURISTIC step (d) with `(C-14)` parenthetical citation; ANTI-PATTERN row updated to name PLS omission |
| **V-04** | C-51 + C-52 | Fully canonical ASR mandate block + one-step-one-operator steps; TCR has 5 rows |
| **V-05** | C-51 + C-52 + C-53 | All three. SCORER HEURISTIC steps (a)-(e), each with single `->` operator; TCR has 6 rows; STRUCTURAL MANDATE (C-49+) has separated independence statement; DCR gains OPEN-ASR code |

### Key structural diffs

**C-51 upgrade** (V-01/V-04/V-05): The `STRUCTURAL MANDATE (C-49+)` block in the ASR section now has all four TCR mandate-template components as independently readable sentences — scope, verification, independence — rather than the R17 pattern where independence was appended as a clause of the verification sentence.

**C-52 upgrade** (V-02/V-04/V-05): R17 step (b) was two sub-lines under one letter. V-02 enforces the contract: one letter = one line = one `->` = one `(C-NN)` pair. A `SCORER HEURISTIC STEP FORMAT` note in the preamble makes the discipline explicit and citable.

**C-53** (V-03/V-05): Confirmed stable from R17. The ANTI-PATTERN row in the canonical template block is extended to explicitly name PLS omission as a collapse condition alongside ASR omission.
