## org-chart Variate — Round 5 Summary

**Baseline:** V-04/R4 (100.0, 12/12 aspirational under v5). R5 asks: is the ceiling axis-robust?

| V | Axis | Key Change | Central Question |
|---|------|-----------|-----------------|

### What Each Variation Risks

- **V-01:** C-14 (mechanism reasons need a prohibition to survive?) and C-16 (named Escalates destination)
- **V-02:** Compliance theater — rote "because of the [X] area" citations that pass C-20's letter but not its intent; also the register section may confuse the eight-section order (now nine sections)
- **V-03:** The known 4-part inertia trap — even with the guard, models may migrate mechanisms. Also C-15 must accommodate a 4th sub-section label
- **V-04:** Register instruction softness under descriptive framing
- **V-05:** Prompt length; C-15 now needs to recognize a 4-part structure while the rubric's three-part sub-section check (C-15) was designed for 3 parts
