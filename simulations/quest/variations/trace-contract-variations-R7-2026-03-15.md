## Round 7 Variation Map

| # | Axis | Single/Combo | Primary Targets |
|---|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **V-01** | output-format | single | **C-22** — skeleton-first in isolation; R6-V-03 passed it only entangled with C-20+C-21 |
| **V-02** | output-format | single | **C-16, C-15** — severity-stratified templates replace unified template; C-15 becomes a structural side effect |
| **V-03** | lifecycle-emphasis | single | **C-13, C-12** — structured parseable gate token `[EXPECTED-SEALED | clauses:N | date:... | author:... | phase:...]` with closure echo |
| **V-04** | output-format × output-format | combo | **C-22 + C-16** — skeleton pre-declares all three tier templates in final position; stratified architecture visible before Phase 1 begins |
| **V-05** | output-format × lifecycle × depth | combo (platinum) | **C-22 + C-16 + C-13 + C-14** — four orthogonal mechanisms: document shape, tier structure, sequencing token, per-finding connector-impact slot on all tiers |

**Key design decisions:**

- **V-02 COSMETIC template** includes `connector-impact` only in V-05 (where it's unconditional on all tiers), not V-02 (where COSMETIC has only 3 fields). This makes C-14 a distinguishing feature of V-05 over V-04.
- **Machine-readable gate** carries a `clauses:{N}` count that is echoed in the `[TRACE-COMPLETE]` closure token — a secondary invariant that makes post-gate additions detectable.
- **V-04 vs V-05**: V-04 adds skeleton + stratified without the gate token or per-finding integration slot, isolating the two-format combination before the platinum push.

**Evaluation order:** V-02 → V-01 → V-03 → V-04 → V-05.
