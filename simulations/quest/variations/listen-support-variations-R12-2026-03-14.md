## listen-support Round 12 — Variations V-01 through V-05

**New criteria targeted**: C-36, C-37, C-38, C-39

**Axes chosen** (3 single, 2 combined):

| V | Axis | Targets |
|---|------|---------|
| V-01 | Step-4 dual-field gate (own-words + verbatim anchor, "both lines" language) | C-37, C-38 |
| V-02 | Exception block 6th field "Paraphrase clause" + no-exception path quotes paraphrase | C-36 |
| V-03 | Inertia framing prominence — competitor name confirmation gate at Steps 4/5/7, 4-body minimum in Part B | none new |
| V-04 | V-01 + V-03 combined | C-37, C-38 |
| V-05 | Verbatim anchor + scope confirmation at Step 4 + exception Paraphrase clause + inertia gates | C-36, C-37, C-39 (not C-38) |

**Key structural diffs:**

- **Step 4 gate**: V-01/V-04 → dual-field (`INFERENCE RULE (confirmed):` + `Verbatim from 2C:`); V-05 → verbatim+scope (`Verbatim anchor from 2C:` + `Confirmation: this rule applies to every severity assignment in the table below.`); V-02/V-03 → free restatement only
- **Exception block**: V-02/V-05 → 6-field with `Paraphrase clause:` + no-exception path quotes governing paraphrase; V-01/V-03/V-04 → 5-field baseline, no-exception path states outcome only
- **Competitor gates**: V-03/V-04/V-05 add mandatory `Competitor in scope:` writes at Steps 4, 5, 7 and raise Part B tool-name fidelity threshold to 4 bodies

**Why V-05 fails C-38**: C-38 requires own-words restatement field AND verbatim anchor simultaneously. V-05 pairs verbatim anchor with scope confirmation instead — the "both lines" guard is present but the two fields are verbatim+scope, not verbatim+own-words. C-37 passes (verbatim anchor present); C-38 does not (no paired restatement field).
