5 complete variations. Each addresses the two new v3 criteria directly.

| Variation | C-13 Ban List | C-14 IA Anchor |
|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| **V-01** | 5-phrase enumerated checklist with `[ ]` boxes in consensus step | Mandatory named `IA anchor:` field in technical role template — explicitly unfillable-by-omission |

- **V-03**: Single axis — phrasing register (conversational "convince the skeptic")
- **V-04**: Combined — lifecycle emphasis (phase gates) + inertia framing (Sub-phase 2A as gate for 2B)
- **V-05**: Combined — formal/terse register + ban list as named numbered artifact
