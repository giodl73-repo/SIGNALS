## Round 16 — Variation Summary

| Variation | Axis | New Criteria Targeted |
|-----------|------|-----------------------|
| **V-01** | Willingness-to-adopt | A-15 (adoption field + 65% threshold), A-16 (Step 0 sub-fields), A-27 (failure mode on Field 1) |
| **V-02** | Statistical depth | A-14 (SD + 95% CI on aggregate), A-20 (±1 swing both directions) |
| **V-03** | Professional lens depth | A-22 (Convergence Statement), A-25 (epistemic UX-before-PM rationale) |
| **V-04** | Numbered manifests + failure modes | A-13, A-23 (card manifest + rule), A-15 (adoption field), A-26 (aggregate manifest + rule), A-27 (2x failure mode annotations on Field 1 and ordering) |
| **V-05** | Inertia delta + conjunctive + cost-tiered | A-16 (Step 0 4 sub-fields), A-21 (conjunctive: Field 2 names Field 3 by label, vice versa), A-24 (each names Step 0 sub-fields by label), A-19 (Low-cost / High-cost tiers in Pass 2), A-25 (UX rationale), A-27 (failure mode on Field 1) |

**All 5 preserve** the full R13 baseline: C-01–C-05, R-01–R-03, A-01–A-12, A-17, A-18, A-20.

**Key differentiators by variation:**

- **V-01** is the cleanest single-axis test of A-15 — adds one new field, defines the 65% threshold explicitly in the protocol, aggregates adoption distribution alongside NPS. Secondary benefit: Step 0 sub-fields hit A-16.

- **V-02** is narrow and surgical — the only change from R13 V-01 baseline is the CI/SD instruction in the aggregate section and ±1 direction clarity. Cleanest test of A-14 in isolation.

- **V-03** restructures only the professional lens section — UX frame moves before personas with an explicit epistemic rationale, PM Read stays after aggregate, and a mandatory Convergence Statement closes the section. No other sections change, making this a clean A-22 + A-25 isolation.

- **V-04** is the maximum-format variation — 9-field numbered persona manifest with completeness rule, 5-field numbered aggregate manifest with completeness rule, and two constraint instructions that explicitly name the failure mode they prevent (Field 1: "gains become feature descriptions"; Field 5/Band: "band cannot be recovered from score alone"; ascending order: "Detractors buried"). Targets A-13, A-23, A-26, A-27 simultaneously.

- **V-05** is the deepest inertia variation — three layers of bilateral enforcement (prose requirement + conjunctive field instructions + named Step 0 sub-field cross-references) make one-sided gains/losses structurally impossible without also suppressing a named baseline sub-field. The Low-cost/High-cost cost tier in Pass 2 is the A-19 target, making revisions actionable by engineering as well as PM.
