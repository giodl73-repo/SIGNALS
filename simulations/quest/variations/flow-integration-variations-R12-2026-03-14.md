### V-01 — C-33 Alternate ARE Subject Form (Q1 probe)
**Axis:** Drops the definite article and positional qualifier from the ARE directive: `"Flag column headers ARE the Obligation Term column values above"` instead of `"The flag column headers in the Stage 1 inventory table ARE..."`.

**Prediction:** 202/202 if any multi-subject ARE satisfies C-33; 197/202 (−5 C-33, C-32 still passes) if exact phrase "the flag column headers" is required.

### V-02 — C-34 Extended Obligation Set, Substituted-Subset Enumeration (Q2 probe)

**Prediction:** 222/222 if per-substitution reading prevails; 212/222 (−5 C-34) or 207/222 (−5 C-27, −5 C-34) if per-table-row reading required.
