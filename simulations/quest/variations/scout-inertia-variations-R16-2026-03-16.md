## Key design decisions

**A-38 new forms under test**:
- `STRUCTURAL FAULTS` (V-02): mirrors confirmed `STRUCTURAL WEAKNESSES` structure; FAULTS is a stronger locus noun (defect planes, not just weakness)
- `FAILURE VECTORS` (V-04): TYPE=failure, LOCUS=vectors — tests directional/pathway metaphor; the FM->Actor and FM->Trigger bridges are literally the downstream ends of each vector
- `COLLAPSE POINTS` (V-05): TYPE=collapse (total breakdown, more severe than FAILURE), LOCUS=points — replaces uncertain R15 V-05 `BREAKING POINTS` (participial form)

**A-39 new form under test**:
- `EACH BRIDGE ARTIFACT VERIFIED?` (V-03): EACH + singular ARTIFACT — tests whether per-element universal quantifier satisfies A-39's "confirms the required COUNT" requirement alongside the confirmed ALL/BOTH + plural ARTIFACTS forms

**A-37**: All five variations are schema-consistent. V-05's R15 SECTION/STAGE mismatch is resolved by converting all section labels to STAGE 1–6 throughout.
