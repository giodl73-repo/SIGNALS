## Key R17 design decisions

**V-03 is a regression probe**, not an advance — it tests whether dropping interrogative headers from V-04's ceiling path collapses C-21/C-26. The answer resolves whether those two criteria are interrogative-text-dependent.

**V-01 and V-04 both include inertia framing** but at different density: V-01 is status-quo-only (no structural adoption), V-04 adds it on top of the confirmed ceiling path. The delta between them isolates inertia's contribution independent of structural adoption.

**V-05's role block** uses 14 vocabulary terms (vs. the standard 10) — including `blue-green cutover`, `circuit-breaker threshold`, `idempotent migration step`, `deployment freeze window`, `environment parity gate` — to push beyond C-11's floor threshold and test whether extended vocabulary depth activates a richness criterion.

**Ceiling sensitivity**: if all three new territory probes (C-09 via inertia, per-phase richness, new inertia criterion) succeed simultaneously in V-05, projected ceiling rises from 175 → 190/220.
