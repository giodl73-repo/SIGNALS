## Key structural choices

All 5 variations preserve every V-02 R4 mechanism:
- 4-field briefing envelope (C-14)
- `### Prior-Stage Lens Impact` in findings + `DOWNSTREAM RISK SHIFT` in handoff (C-16)
- "do not copy the lens impact into the risk shift" explicit guard (C-17)

The only variable is **where and how** the `Lens:` declaration field appears. Stage 1 (no briefing envelope) receives a standalone `Lens:` field in Stage Identity across all variations. V-05 also adds the per-stage `INERTIA EVOLUTION` field and end-of-run `INERTIA TRAJECTORY` section from V-03 R4, making it the ceiling candidate for potential C-19 elevation.
