# Quest Variate -- /signal (Round 3)

**5 variations targeting C-14 (bi-directional descriptions) and C-15 (mutually distinguishable taglines).**

---

## Variation Summary

| Variation | Axis | Hypothesis |
|-----------|------|------------|
| **V-01** | C-14 single-axis | Explicit BIDIR RULE ("mechanism verb phrase -> quantified deliverable") forces both dimensions into every description |
| **V-02** | C-15 single-axis | Explicit SWAP TEST ("could this tagline apply to any other namespace?") forces namespace-locked taglines |
| **V-03** | C-14 + C-15 combined | Both new rules together confirm no interference — they operate at different output levels (description vs header) |
| **V-04** | Full integration | R2 V-05 skeleton + RULE 6 (C-14) + RULE 7 (C-15) = all 7 aspirational criteria enforced by explicit rules |
| **V-05** | Structural pre-print | Pre-printed `[verb phrase] -> [deliverable]` format and uniqueness-anchored taglines make C-14/C-15 structurally impossible to violate — model transcribes, not generates |

---

## Key Design Decisions

**V-01 BIDIR RULE format**: `[mechanism] -> [deliverable]` with explicit PASS/FAIL examples. The `->` separator is the structural anchor — it forces the model to produce both halves.

**V-02 SWAP TEST**: Taglines are evaluated against a "could this move to another namespace?" test. The rule makes uniqueness operational. Examples: "predict what customers will say before they say it" is locked to listen; "state a hypothesis then build an evidence case" is locked to prove.

**V-04 vs V-05 structural difference**: V-04 adds two rules to free-form output. V-05 eliminates the rules by pre-printing the full data — the model's only job is accurate transcription. If the pre-print scores higher than V-04, structural enforcement dominates over rule enforcement.

**R2 base preserved**: All five R2 format rules (C-09 through C-13) are carried in every variation. The routing guide and bare-mode order contract from R2 V-05 are present in V-01 through V-04. V-05 simplifies to 5 consolidated rules to reduce cognitive overhead.
