## R12 Summary

Three new criteria from v11 rubric, applied to all 5 variations:

| Criterion | What changed across all variations |
|-----------|-----------------------------------|
| **C-31** | Phase 1a register upgraded to 4-column schema: `FID / Finding Summary / Primary Competitor / Switching Cost (H/M/L)` |
| **C-32** | Phase 5 Counter Block now carries its own bold label and schema `FID / Counter Claim / Rebuttal`, structurally separated from Gap Block with an explicit note distinguishing the two |
| **C-33** | H/M/L confidence labels annotated with quantified thresholds everywhere they appear — H = 80%+, M = 50–79%, L < 50% |

**Variation axes:**

|-----------|------|------------|

| **V-04** | C-31 + C-32 combined | Both emphasis axes coexist without crowding |
| **V-05** | All three + full structural compliance | All criteria embedded in headers/schema; targets 102.5/102.5 with BECAUSE 6-slot, traffic-light severity, segment fit scores, and C-09 Confidence Rationale column |
