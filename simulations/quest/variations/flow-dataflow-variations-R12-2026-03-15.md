## Round 12 — flow-dataflow Variations (Rubric v10)

Five complete variations across three single axes and two combinations:

### Variation Design

R12 operates on a closed rubric (v10 = v9 validated). The goal is axis-robustness testing: does the 17/17 result from R11 V-01 hold under register softening, lifecycle reordering, and inertia-reframing?

| Variation | Axis | Domain | Key Hypothesis |
|-----------|------|--------|----------------|
| **V-01** | Phrasing register: conversational/question-driven | Finance — AP invoice automation | Scaffold produced but upstream-references column sparse; C-24 partial pass |
| **V-02** | Lifecycle: boundary-first (T-03 stub before stages) | Operations — Supplier ASN → DC receiving | Boundary pre-commitment drives C-11 completeness; C-20 field citations depend on whether model returns to fill stubs |
| **V-03** | Inertia framing: incumbent-centric (INCUMBENT EQUIVALENT block per stage) | Commerce — ERP→storefront catalog sync | Per-stage MS-NN co-location drives full C-16 density; recovery framed as "revert to MS-NN" |
| **V-04** | Combined: conversational + boundary-first | Finance — payroll processing | Axes may partially cancel; boundary stubs add structural pressure the soft register removes |
| **V-05** | Combined: scaffold-first + inertia-first + 4-pass roles (Scaffold Architect → Operations → Finance → Commerce) | Operations/Finance/Commerce — multi-tier inventory sync | Cross-role T-01 citation forces C-16 traceability without within-role fallback; strongest C-14/C-15/C-16 test design to date |

### New Signal Candidates

1. **Phrasing-register scaffold decay** (V-01, V-04) — does softening "MUST list T-NN tokens" to "could you list..." collapse upstream-references to "none"?
2. **Boundary pre-commitment fill rate** (V-02, V-04) — does the model return to update T-03 entity-at-risk stubs with `entity.field_name` after producing exit manifests?
3. **Incumbent-centric C-16 density** (V-03, V-05) — does per-stage INCUMBENT EQUIVALENT block drive every recovery row to carry an MS-NN step citation vs the "at least one" baseline?
4. **Cross-role T-01 citation quality** (V-05) — does Commerce accurately cite Operations-produced MS-NN steps without having generated T-01 itself?
