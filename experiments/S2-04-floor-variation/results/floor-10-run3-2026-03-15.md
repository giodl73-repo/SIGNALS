## Competitive Brief: Signal Plugin

**Strategic lead:** Signal has one uncontested position -- the pre-build evidence layer inside the developer's own repo. Every named competitor activates after the build decision is made, or lives outside the IDE entirely.

---

### Primary Competitor: Inertia (Threat: HIGH)

Inertia wins by default. Teams skip Signal because:
- Sprint planning commits the work before anyone asks "should we?"
- The cost of skipping is deferred -- invisible on the day the decision is made
- Velocity culture actively penalizes pausing to investigate
- Existing rituals (design review, arch review) already fill the pre-build slot
- **Survivorship bias**: teams that skip once and ship fine conclude Signal isn't needed

The hardest objection to overcome: *"We already do this informally."*

### The Whitespace Signal Owns

> Structured, multi-namespace evidence accumulation that lives as repo-native artifacts, runs inside the developer's IDE, crosses the PM-dev boundary, and synthesizes per-topic into a decision narrative.

No named competitor owns all four of: repo-native + developer-facing + multi-axis (9 namespaces) + topic narrative layer.

### --mode risk (Exec Frame)

*"Signal is not a research tool. It is a rework prevention system. The choice is not: investigate or skip. The choice is: investigate now (hours) or investigate later (weeks, in production, at 10x cost)."*
