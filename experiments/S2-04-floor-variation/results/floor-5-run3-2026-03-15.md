## Competitive Brief: Signal Plugin

**Strategic insight**: Signal occupies **the evidence layer before the commit** — a gap no competitor has claimed. Every named competitor is optimized for after the decision.

---

### Finding 1 — Inertia (Primary Competitor) | Threat: HIGH

The real competitor is teams doing pre-development work with existing tools — ChatGPT conversations, Confluence brain-dumps, Jira epics — and believing that counts as investigation. Inertia is sticky because:
- The cost lands in sprint retros, not on the decision
- A 30-minute AI conversation feels like research
- No artifact trail means nothing to audit when the spec changes

---

### Finding 2 — The Whitespace (Uncontested Ground)

No tool owns **structured, namespaced, repo-first signal gathering as a discipline**. No competitor:
- Produces a topic story synthesizing signals across all tracks
- Structures evidence by audience namespace (PM / dev / researcher)
- Provides amendment tracking from hypothesis to spec update
- Runs 9 coordinated investigation tracks with a convergence check

---

### Finding 3 — Table Stakes

Any entrant must: work inside the dev environment, produce repo-resident artifacts, be CLI-first with no signup, output spec-feeding findings (not reports), and cost nothing to try. Signal meets all five. Copilot, Jira PD, and Notion AI each fail at least two.

---

### Finding 4 — Named Competitors

| Competitor | Overlap | Threat | Key Gap |
|---|---|---|---|
| **GitHub Copilot** (Plan Mode) | MEDIUM | HIGH | Issue-to-code, not hypothesis-to-spec. No scout/trace/prove tracks. Distribution advantage: 150M accounts. Source: [GitHub Docs](https://docs.github.com/en/copilot/get-started/features) |
| **Cursor** (Plan Mode + Automations) | MEDIUM-HIGH | HIGH | Reacts to events that happened; no pre-decision investigation. 35% of PRs from agents. Source: [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/05/cursor-is-rolling-out-a-new-system-for-agentic-coding/) |
| **Jira Product Discovery** | LOW | MEDIUM | PM-facing, ticket-centric, no repo artifacts. Sales objection risk at Atlassian shops. Source: [Atlassian](https://www.atlassian.com/software/jira/product-discovery/features) |
| **Notion AI** | LOW | LOW-MEDIUM | Doc-centric, not repo-centric. No trace or prove tracks. Bottom-up SaaS threat for spec-writing step only. Source: [Landmark Labs](https://www.landmarklabs.co/block/competitor-research-notion-ai) |
| **DIY Claude Code Workflows** | HIGH | HIGH | Same runtime, zero moat, but no namespace ontology, no golden prompts, no convergence check. Community is building partial substitutes now. Sources: [shinpr/claude-code-workflows](https://github.com/shinpr/claude-code-workflows), [ComposioHQ/awesome-claude-plugins](https://github.com/ComposioHQ/awesome-claude-plugins) |

---

### Finding 5 — `--mode risk`: Cost of Building the Wrong Thing

For exec audiences: Signal is a **risk reduction tool**, not a productivity tool.

- Mid-cycle pivot → sprint lost (Signal surfaces it day 1)
- Spec gap → contract violation found post-implementation (costs zero if caught by `/trace:contract` pre-spec)
- Stakeholder revision cycle → 40 person-hours per cycle for a 5-person team
- Feature abandonment → the most expensive outcome; `/listen:adoption` + `/prove:interview` exist to prevent it

The right question: *"What did the last wrong feature cost you?"*

---

### AMEND — 3 Adjustments

1. **Narrow to Claude Code plugin ecosystem only** → removes Jira/Notion/Cursor; shows Signal has zero competition inside its own distribution channel
2. **Add Perplexity / ChatGPT Research Mode** → captures the "I already research with AI" objection more precisely than the inertia framing
3. **Add time-to-first-signal to Table Stakes** → "usable artifact in under 5 minutes on first run" as a quantified bar
