## Competitive Brief: Signal Plugin

**15 findings (F-01 – F-15)** across 5 sections.

### Primary Competitor — Inertia (threat: HIGH)

- **F-01**: Inertia is active and socially reinforced, not passive. Teams that have always shipped without evidence-gathering have no reason to change — Signal must beat "doing nothing" on speed, not features.
- **F-02**: The METR study (July 2025) found developers took 19% longer than they perceived with AI tools — parallel failure mode. Signal's job is to make the cost of skipping visible before it's incurred.

### The Whitespace

- **F-03**: No competitor frames the problem as "help me know what I know before I commit." Every other tool says "write code faster" or "track work." That reframe is the moat.

### Table Stakes

- **F-04**: Zero-setup first run is a filter that eliminates most research tools from developer consideration. Configuration-before-trust means developers never reach the first artifact.

### Risk Mode (exec frame)

- **F-14**: Signal addresses "are we building the right thing?" — not "will the code work?" Exec number: cost of a 6-week sprint on the wrong feature.
- **F-15**: Signal artifacts are a decision audit trail. In regulated industries, that trail is a compliance requirement. `/prove:publish` and `/scout:compliance` address this directly.
