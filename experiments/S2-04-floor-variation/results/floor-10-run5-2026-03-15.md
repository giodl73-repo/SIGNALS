## Competitive Brief: Signal Plugin — Summary

### The Strategic Insight

Signal operates in a layer that doesn't exist as a named category yet: **pre-spec evidence gathering for developers**. Every named competitor works either above (PM discovery, workspace AI) or below (spec validation, feature flags, analytics). The gap between "someone has an idea" and "the spec is written" is uncontested. That's Signal's territory.

---

### Primary Competitor: Inertia — **Threat: HIGH**

Three sticky patterns keep teams doing nothing:
1. *"We'll iterate"* — Agile culture normalizes launching wrong
2. *"We can just ask ChatGPT"* — unstructured LLM output feels like evidence
3. *"We already have a spec process"* — teams don't miss what they've never had

---

### The Whitespace

```
[Idea] ---> [ ??? ] ---> [Spec] ---> [Build] ---> [Ship] ---> [Measure]
              ^
              Signal's uncontested territory
```

No competitor owns this. PM tools require user access. SDD tools assume the spec exists. Workspace AI documents decisions, doesn't challenge them. Feature flags are post-commit.
