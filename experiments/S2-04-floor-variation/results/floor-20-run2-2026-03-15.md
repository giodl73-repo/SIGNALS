# Competitive Brief: Signal Plugin

**Strategic lead**: Signal has one uncontested position — the pre-build evidence layer inside the developer's own repo. Every named competitor either activates after the decision to build, or lives outside the IDE. The real threats: (1) Anthropic's /feature-dev (adjacent execution-phase), (2) GitHub Copilot Workspace (pre-build spec at GitHub scale), (3) raw inertia.

---

## Primary Competitor: Inertia

**Threat: HIGH (always)**

- F-01: Inertia is not passivity — it is an active, habitual, socially reinforced choice. Signal wins by being faster than "is this worth the time?" not by being more comprehensive.
- F-02: The METR study (July 2025) found developers took 19% longer than they perceived. Signal's job is to make the cost of skipping visible before it is incurred.
- F-03: "We already do this informally" is the most dangerous objection. Signal needs a demo that surfaces a gap the informal version misses — a P1 from `/trace:permissions` or `/flow:throttle` that a PM brief never mentions.
